# Comparative Adjective Prompt Cards -- Draft

Use these cards to prompt students to make comparative sentences.

## Card 1
Compare: football and basketball
Adjective: popular
Example frame: ______ is more popular than ______ in Turkey.

## Card 2
Compare: summer and winter
Adjective: hot
Example frame: Summer is ______ than winter.

## Card 3
Compare: cats and dogs
Adjective: independent
Example frame: Cats are ______ than dogs.

## Card 4
Compare: a Ferrari and a bicycle
Adjective: expensive
Example frame: A Ferrari is ______ than a bicycle.

## Card 5
Compare: the Mediterranean Sea and the Black Sea
Adjective: warm
Example frame: The Mediterranean Sea is ______ than the Black Sea.

## Card 6
Compare: living in a city and living in a village
Adjective: stressful
Example frame: Living in a city is ______ than living in a village.

## Card 7
Compare: English grammar and Turkish grammar
Adjective: easy / difficult
Example frame: ______ grammar is ______ than ______ grammar.

## Card 8
Compare: going to a restaurant and cooking at home
Adjective: cheap
Example frame: Cooking at home is ______ than going to a restaurant.
