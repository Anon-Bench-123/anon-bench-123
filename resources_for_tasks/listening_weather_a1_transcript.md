# Weather Forecast - Audio Transcript

**Level:** A1
**Context:** Young learners aged 10-12 in South Africa
**Duration:** 1.5 minutes
**Format:** Radio weather report

---

## Audio Transcript

**Presenter:** Good morning! This is Radio South Africa with your weather forecast for today, Friday the 15th of March.

Today will be a beautiful sunny day in Johannesburg! The temperature will be 25 degrees Celsius. It's perfect weather to play outside!

In Cape Town, it will be cloudy in the morning, but the sun will come out in the afternoon. The temperature will be 22 degrees. Don't forget your jacket in the morning!

Durban will have rain today. It will be wet and windy. The temperature will be only 18 degrees. Remember to take your umbrella and wear your raincoat!

In Pretoria, it will be very hot and sunny. The temperature will reach 28 degrees! Drink lots of water and wear your hat and sunglasses.

And now, what should you wear today?

In Johannesburg and Pretoria: Wear light clothes like a t-shirt and shorts. Don't forget your hat and sunscreen!

In Cape Town: Wear a light jacket in the morning. You can take it off in the afternoon!

In Durban: Wear long trousers and a warm jumper. Take your umbrella and raincoat!

That's all for today's weather forecast. Have a great day, everyone!
