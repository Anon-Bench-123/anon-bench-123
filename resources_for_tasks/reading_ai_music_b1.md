Using AI in Music 

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing how music is created, produced, and shared. Musicians now use AI tools to help write songs, compose melodies, and even generate lyrics. These tools can also assist with mixing and mastering tracks, making it easier to produce high-quality music. Streaming platforms like Spotify use AI to recommend songs and analyze trends to help artists grow their audience. 

In music composition, AI can generate full songs, chord progressions, and basslines based on a user’s style or mood. It can also help songwriters overcome creative blocks by suggesting lyrics or new ideas. Some AI programs can even recreate instrument sounds or produce vocals that sound like real singers. 

AI also supports music production by improving sound quality and restoring old recordings. For example, AI was used to clean up audio for The Beatles’ song “Now And Then.” It can separate vocals from instruments and automate technical tasks, saving time for producers. 

Finally, AI is making music creation more accessible. People without musical training can now create songs using simple tools. While some worry AI might replace human creativity, many believe it’s a helpful partner that inspires new ideas and makes music more democratic.
