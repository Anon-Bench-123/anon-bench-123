# Speaking Presentation Rubric -- B2 Level

**Assessment type**: End-of-semester summative speaking test
**Task**: Individual presentation (4-5 minutes)

---

## Scoring Grid

Each criterion is scored 1-5. Total possible: 25 marks.

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 5 | Excellent -- exceeds expectations for B2 |
| 4 | Good -- meets B2 expectations fully |
| 3 | Satisfactory -- meets B2 expectations with some weaknesses |
| 2 | Below expectations -- significant weaknesses for B2 |
| 1 | Inadequate -- well below B2 expectations |

---

## Criteria

### 1. Content and Task Achievement (out of 5)
- Presentation addresses the topic fully with clear, relevant ideas
- Includes supporting detail, examples, or evidence
- Meets the time requirement (4-5 minutes)

### 2. Organisation and Coherence (out of 5)
- Clear introduction, body, and conclusion
- Ideas are logically sequenced
- Effective use of signposting and transitions between sections

### 3. Grammatical Range and Accuracy (out of 5)
- Uses a range of grammatical structures appropriate for B2
- Errors are infrequent and do not impede understanding
- Demonstrates control of complex structures

### 4. Vocabulary Range and Appropriacy (out of 5)
- Uses varied and topic-appropriate vocabulary
- Can express ideas without noticeable searching for words
- Vocabulary is accurate and natural for the context

### 5. Delivery and Fluency (out of 5)
- Speaks at a natural pace with minimal hesitation
- Can sustain the presentation without reading from notes
- Appropriate volume, eye contact, and engagement with audience
