# Speaking Assessment Transcript

**Student**: Yuto, age 6
**Task**: Look at the pictures of animals and say what you can see. Say if you like the animal.
**Context**: End-of-unit speaking check, A1 class (pre-primary)

---

## Transcript

**Teacher**: (shows picture of a dog) Yuto, what can you see?

**Yuto**: Dog! A dog.

**Teacher**: Very good. Do you like dogs?

**Yuto**: Yes. I like dog. I have... my house... dog. (gestures with hands to show small size) Small.

**Teacher**: (shows picture of a cat) And what about this one?

**Yuto**: Cat. This is cat. Um... I no like cat. Cat is... (makes scratching gesture) ... itai... um, scary.

**Teacher**: (shows picture of a fish) OK, and this animal?

**Yuto**: (long pause) ... Fish? Yes, fish. Fish is... (moves hand in swimming motion) ... swim. I like fish. My fish is... colour is... orange.

**Teacher**: (shows picture of an elephant) And the last one?

**Yuto**: Erefanto... no... (thinks) ... elephant! Big! Very big. I like. I see... um... (points around as if indicating a place) ... doubutsuen... I see elephant.

**Teacher**: Great, thank you Yuto!

---

**Duration**: Approximately 2 minutes
**Notes**: Student relied on gestures and occasional L1 (Japanese) words. Required teacher prompting for each item.
