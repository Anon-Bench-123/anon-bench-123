# Speaking Assessment: Basic Introduction

**Student**: Young learner, aged 5-7
**Task**: Simple oral task - say name, age, and favorite color
**Context**: Very young learners in Lusaka, Zambia, A1 level

---

## Student Performance Transcript

**Teacher**: Hello! Can you tell me your name?

**Student**: My name is... um... Chipo.

**Teacher**: Very good! How old are you, Chipo?

**Student**: I am... (counts on fingers)... I am six.

**Teacher**: Wonderful! And what is your favorite color?

**Student**: Blue! I like blue.

**Teacher**: That's great! Thank you, Chipo.

---

**Duration**: Less than 1 minute
**Assessment**:
- **Learning goal achieved**: YES - student successfully provided all three pieces of information (name, age, favorite color)
- **Notes**: Age-appropriate hesitation, needed to count on fingers for age (normal for this age), clear pronunciation of key words, responded appropriately to questions
