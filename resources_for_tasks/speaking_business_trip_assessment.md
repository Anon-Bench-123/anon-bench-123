# Speaking Assessment: Business Trip Presentation

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Give a 3-minute presentation about a recent business trip

---

## Student Performance Transcript

Last month I went to Dubai for a business meeting. The meeting was about a new project for our company. I presented our proposal to the clients and I think it went well because they asked many questions and they seemed interested. The hotel was very nice and I had some free time to visit the mall and the beach. The only problem was the traffic which was very bad. I learned that it is important to prepare well before a presentation because the clients will notice if you are not ready. Next time I will practice more before I go.
