# Speaking Assessment: Advantages and Disadvantages of City Living

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Answer the question "What are the advantages and disadvantages of living in a big city?" as practice for the Cambridge B2 First Speaking exam

---

## Student Work

Well, living in a big city has many advantages and disadvantages. On one hand, in a city you can find many opportunities for work and for the study. There are also many cultural activities like museums, theatres and concerts. The public transport is usually good so you dont need a car.

On the other hand, the life in a city can be very stressful. There is too much traffic and pollution. Also the cost of living is much more higher than in a small town. Sometimes people in cities they feel lonely because they dont know their neighbours.

Personally I prefer to live in a big city because I like to have many things to do, but I understand people who prefer the countryside for a more peaceful life.
