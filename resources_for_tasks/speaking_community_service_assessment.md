# Speaking Assessment: Paired Discussion on Community Service

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Discuss in pairs whether young people should be required to do community service (oral exam)

---

## Student Performance Transcript

I think community service is a very good idea for young people because it teaches them responsibility and they can learn about the real problems in society. For example, if a student works in a hospital or a care home, they will understand how important these jobs are. However, I am not sure if it should be required because some students already have many responsibilities with school and part-time jobs. Maybe it would be better to offer it as an optional activity with some kind of reward, like extra credit for university applications. What do you think about that?
