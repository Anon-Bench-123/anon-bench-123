# Speaking Assessment: Handling Guest Complaint

**Student**: B1 adult, aged 20-30, service industry worker
**Task**: Role-play handling difficult hotel guest complaint
**Context**: English for service industry in Tunis, Tunisia

---

## Role-Play Transcript

**Guest (played by another student)**: Excuse me! I need to speak to someone about my room. This is completely unacceptable!

**Student**: Good afternoon, sir. I am sorry that you are having problem. Can you please tell me what is wrong?

**Guest**: What's wrong? The air conditioning doesn't work, the wifi keeps disconnecting, and when I called the front desk 30 minutes ago, nobody came to help!

**Student**: I am really sorry to hear this, sir. This is not the service we want to give to our guests. Let me help you to solve these problems. First, can I ask what is your room number?

**Guest**: Room 305.

**Student**: Thank you. Room 305. Now, for the air conditioning, I will send our technician immediately to check it. He should arrive in your room in the next 10 to 15 minutes. Is that okay for you?

**Guest**: Well, it should have been fixed hours ago, but yes, I suppose that's fine.

**Student**: I understand your frustration, sir, and I apologize again. For the wifi problem, sometimes the signal can be weak in some rooms. I can offer you a portable wifi device for your stay, which will give you stronger connection. Would that help you?

**Guest**: Yes, that would help. But what about the fact that nobody responded to my call earlier?

**Student**: You are absolutely right to be upset about this. This should not happen. I will speak to my manager about this to make sure it don't happen again. And sir, because of these problems and the inconvenience for you, I would like to offer you a complimentary dinner in our restaurant tonight. Would you accept this?

**Guest**: Well... that's better. But I really expect better service for what I'm paying.

**Student**: You are completely right, sir. We want you to have excellent experience here. I promise that we will fix all these problems quickly. I will personally come to check your room in one hour to make sure everything is working well. Is there anything else I can help you with?

**Guest**: No, I think that covers everything. Thank you for actually trying to help.

**Student**: It is my pleasure, sir. Again, I apologize for the problems. If you need anything else, please call me directly. My name is Ahmed and this is my extension number. Thank you for your patience.
