# Daily Routine Dialogue - Audio Transcript

**Level:** A1
**Context:** Young learners aged 9-11 in Nigeria
**Duration:** 2 minutes
**Characters:** Emma (girl, 10 years old) and her mother

---

## Audio Dialogue Transcript

**Mother:** Good morning, Emma! Time to wake up!

**Emma:** Good morning, Mummy. What time is it?

**Mother:** It's seven o'clock. You need to get ready for school.

**Emma:** OK, Mummy.

**[Sound effect: water running]**

**Mother:** Emma, what are you doing now?

**Emma:** I'm brushing my teeth, Mummy.

**Mother:** Good girl! Come and have breakfast after that.

**[Sound effect: footsteps, chair scraping]**

**Emma:** Mummy, what's for breakfast?

**Mother:** Bread and eggs. And orange juice.

**Emma:** Thank you! I'm hungry!

**Mother:** Emma, you need to get dressed after breakfast.

**Emma:** Yes, Mummy. I'm wearing my school uniform.

**[Sound effect: school bell ringing in distance]**

**Mother:** Hurry, Emma! It's half past seven. School starts at eight o'clock.

**Emma:** I'm putting on my shoes now!

**Mother:** Don't forget your schoolbag!

**Emma:** I have it, Mummy. My books are inside.

**Mother:** Do you have your lunch box?

**Emma:** Yes! And my water bottle!

**Mother:** Very good. Time to go to school now.

**Emma:** Goodbye, Mummy!

**Mother:** Goodbye, Emma! Have a good day at school!

**Emma:** Thank you, Mummy!
