# Speaking Sample: Interview Transcript

**Student**: student-1, Age 20, Adult General English course
**Task**: The examiner asks the student about their daily routine, their hobbies, and their plans for the weekend.

---

## Transcript of Student's Response

**Examiner:** Can you tell me about your typical day?

**Student:** Yes, um, I wake up at seven o'clock every day. I have breakfast with my family, usually bread and cheese and tea. Then I go to the university. I study engineering. After the classes I sometimes go to the library for study. In the evening I come to home and I eat dinner. After dinner I watch TV or I use my phone. I sleep at eleven usually.

**Examiner:** What do you do in your free time?

**Student:** I like playing football very much. I play football with my friends in the park, two times in a week. Also I like to cooking. I learn new recipes from the YouTube. Last month I cook a very delicious pasta. My family liked it very much. Sometimes I read book but not very often because I am tired from the university.

**Examiner:** What are you going to do this weekend?

**Student:** This weekend I will go to Istanbul with my friend. We will visit my cousin who is living there. We will go to shopping and maybe we go to the Bosphorus for walking. I am very excited because I don't went to Istanbul for long time. I think we will have very good time.
