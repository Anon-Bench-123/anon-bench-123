# Speaking Sample: Debate Transcript (Excerpt)

**Student**: student-1, age 17
**Task**: Participate in a class debate arguing FOR the statement "Technology in the classroom does more harm than good" (3-minute excerpt from a longer debate)

---

## Student's Transcript

OK so I think that technology in the classroom is actually a big problem and I will explain you why. First of all, we can see that students who use tablets and computers in class they are more distracted than students who use only books. There are many studies that prove this. For example, I have read one study that say that students who use laptops in class they take worse notes because they just copy everything without thinking.

Also, I think we need to consider about the social effects. When students are always looking at their screens, they do not talk to each other. In my own class, I have noticed that when the teacher gives us a group activity on the computer, everyone just looks at their own screen instead of discuss together. Before the technology, we used to have much more interesting conversations in class.

Another point I would like to mention is the cost. Many schools in Mexico they cannot afford to buy computers for all the students. So what happens is that some students have technology and some do not, and this creates a big inequality. The government should to invest more money in training teachers instead of buying expensive devices that will be obsolete in two or three years.

So to summarise my arguments, I believe that although technology can be useful sometimes, in the classroom it is causing more problems than it solves. The schools should to focus more on good teaching and less on technology. Thank you.
