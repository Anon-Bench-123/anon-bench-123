# Speaking Sample: Debate Excerpt (Transcript)

**Student**: student-1, age 17, Upper-Secondary English class
**Task**: Participate in a 2-minute debate arguing against the statement "University education should be free for everyone." The student was given 1 minute to prepare.

## Student's Transcript

OK so I am going to argue that university should not be free for everyone and I have some reasons for this.

First of all, if university is free, the government needs to pay for it and this money comes from taxes. So actually it is not really free, someone is still paying. And maybe the people who is paying taxes, they did not go to university themselves, so why should they pay for other people to go? I think this is not fair.

My second point is about quality. When you look at countries where university is free, sometimes the universities they are very crowd because everyone wants to go. And when you have too many students, the quality of education it goes down. The professors cannot give attention to every student. I think it is better to have less students who really want to study.

Also I want to say that when you pay for something, you value it more. If a student pays for university, they will study more harder because they don't want to waste their money. But if it is free, maybe some students they go just because it is free and they don't take it serious.

However, I agree that there should be scholarships for students who cannot afford. The government can help these students but not make it free for everyone.

So in my conclusion, I believe that university should not be free because it costs too much for taxpayers, it reduces quality, and students don't value it enough. Thank you.
