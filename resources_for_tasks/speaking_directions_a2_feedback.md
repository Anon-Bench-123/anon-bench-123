# Speaking Sample: Giving Directions

**Student**: student-1, age 28, Adult
**Task**: Your teacher asked you to record yourself giving directions from your home to your workplace. Describe the route as clearly as you can.

---

## Student's Transcript

OK so... from my house I go... I go in the street and I turn to the left. Then I walk maybe... five minutes? And I arrive to the bus stop. The bus stop is in front of a big supermarket, you know, the Carrefour. I take the bus number 12 and I go... about twenty minutes in the bus.

When I arrive... I get down from the bus in the centre of the city. Then I must to walk again. I go straight and... how you say... I cross the big road, the road with many cars. After I turn in the right and I walk past a pharmacy and a bakery. The bakery have very good bread, I always buy bread there in the morning.

Then I see my office, it is a building very tall with... with glass, you know, the modern building. I enter to the building and I take the elevator to the floor five. My office is the second door on the left. Usually the travel from my house take... maybe forty-five minutes or one hour, it depends of the traffic. When it rains the bus is always very slow because there is many cars. In the summer it is better because I can to walk more and take a short way.
