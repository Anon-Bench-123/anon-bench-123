# Speaking Assessment: Describing Favorite Place

**Student**: B1 level from Cambodia
**Task**: Speaking test - describe favorite place in Cambodia
**Context**: Preparing for final exam

---

## Student Performance Transcript

My favorite place in Cambodia is Angkor Wat. It is very famous temple in Siem Reap. I visit there many times with my family and friends.

Angkor Wat is very old. It was built in... I think 12th century. The temple is very big and beautiful. It has five towers and many stone carvings on the walls. The carvings tell stories from Hindu religion.

When I go to Angkor Wat, I like to go early in the morning, before sunrise. Many tourists come to watch the sunrise behind the temple. It is very beautiful view. The sky becomes orange and red, and you can see the reflection of temple in the water.

Inside the temple, there are many corridors and stairs. Some stairs are very steep, so you must be careful when you climb. From the top, you can see all the trees and other temples around.

I like this place because it makes me feel proud of Cambodian history. When I see Angkor Wat, I remember that Cambodian people built this amazing temple many hundreds years ago. It shows that our ancestors were very skilled and creative.

Also, the area around Angkor Wat is very peaceful. There are big trees and sometimes you can see monkeys. It is good place to walk and think.

Every time I bring foreign friends to visit, they are very impressed. They say it is one of the most beautiful places they have seen. This makes me happy because I can share my culture with them.

For me, Angkor Wat is not just tourist attraction. It is symbol of Cambodia and it connects me to my history and identity.
