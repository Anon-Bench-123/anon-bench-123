In the first photo, the students are learning in a group in a traditional academic environment, while in the second photo, the woman is learning independently, probably at home. I think the students might feel pressure because they have deadlines and they need to collaborate with others, but the woman probably feels more relaxed because she can learn at her own speed.

Both photos show that learning is important at any age, and people today have many different ways to study - both in classroom with others or at home by themselves. I think the students might be doing more formal education, like university degree, while the woman is maybe doing more personal development, learning something for her own interest or for work.
