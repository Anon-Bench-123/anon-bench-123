# Speaking Assessment: Favourite Sport Talk

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Give a short talk about their favourite sport (recorded and transcribed)

---

## Student Performance Transcript

Football is my favourite sport. I play football every day with my friends after school. My favourite team is Real Madrid because they have very good players. I like Vinicius Junior the most because he is very fast and he can score many goals. Last year I watched a match on TV and it was very exciting. My father also likes football and sometimes we go to the stadium together. I want to become a professional footballer when I grow up but my mother says I should study hard first. I think she is right but I still want to try.
