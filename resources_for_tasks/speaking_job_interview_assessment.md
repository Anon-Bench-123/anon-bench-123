# Speaking Assessment: Job Interview Mock

**Student**: B2 level, aged 20-25
**Task**: 5-minute mock interview response about strengths and career goals
**Context**: Language institute in Jakarta, Indonesia - preparing for job interviews

---

## Student Performance Transcript

Thank you for the opportunity to interview today. I would like to tell you about my strengths and my career goals.

First, about my strengths. I think my biggest strength is I am very good at communicate with people. In my university, I was the leader of student organization, so I have experience to work with many different people and solve the problems together. I can work in team very well. Also, I am organized person. I always make plan before I start project and I follow the schedule carefully.

Another strength is I am quick learner. When I did internship last year at marketing company, I learn how to use new software in just two weeks, and my supervisor said I did very good job. I think this is important strength for job because technology always changing and we need to adapt quickly.

For my career goals, I want to build my career in marketing field, especially digital marketing. I am very interested in social media marketing and how companies can use it to reach customers. In five years, I hope I can become marketing manager and lead a team. I want to develop my skills in data analysis and strategy planning.

I choose to apply for this position because I believe your company can give me opportunity to learn and grow. I have researched about your company and I am impressed with your innovative campaigns. I think I can contribute my creativity and my communication skills to help your team success.

I am also planning to take some professional certifications in digital marketing next year to improve my knowledge. I believe continuous learning is very important for career development.

That is about my strengths and career goals. Do you have any questions for me?

---

**Duration**: Approximately 4 minutes 30 seconds
**Delivery**: Moderate pace, fairly fluent with occasional pauses to find words
**Strengths**: Good content, clear organization, appropriate vocabulary for B2 level
**Areas for improvement**: Some grammatical errors (communicate/communicating, solve the problems/solve problems), occasional article errors, some word choice issues (did very good/did a very good job)
