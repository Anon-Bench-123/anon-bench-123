# Speaking Assessment: Memorable Journey Talk

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Give a 3-minute talk about a memorable journey

---

## Student Work

Last year I went to Samarkand with my family. It was very amazing trip. We visited Registan square and I was very impress by the architecture. The buildings are very old, from maybe 14th century. My father he told us many stories about the history. We also tried local food which was very delicious. I think every person should visit Samarkand because it is a very beautiful place. The weather was perfect, not too hot and not too cold. We stayed three days and I wish we could stay more longer.
