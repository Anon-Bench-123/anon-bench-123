# Speaking Sample: News Talk Transcript

**Student**: student-1, age 16, Upper-Secondary
**Task**: Give a two-minute talk about a news topic you find interesting

---

## Student's Transcript

Today I want to talk about artificial intelligence in education. This is topic that I find very interesting because it affects all of us as students.

Firstly, AI is important tool for education. Students can get help from computer at any time, not only during school hours. For example, there are programs that can check your writing and give you suggestions. I think this is very useful especially for students who need extra help.

On the other hand, some people think it is bad for learning. They say that if students will use AI too much, they will stop thinking by themselves. I understand this point but I believe that technology can be used in good way if teachers show students how to use it properly.

Another thing I want to mention is that AI can do many good things for students with different levels. Computer can give different exercises to different students based on their level. This means that everyone can learn at their own speed.

In future, technology will change classroom completely. Schools need to prepare for this. If schools will invest in AI, students will learn better. But it is important that we do not forget about human contact between teacher and student.

To sum up, I think AI is great opportunity for education but we need to use it carefully. Thank you for listening.
