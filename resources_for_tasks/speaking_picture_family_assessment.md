# Speaking Assessment: Picture Description - Family at a Park

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Describe a picture of a family at a park (speaking test)

---

## Student Performance Transcript

In the picture I can see a family. There is a mother and a father and two children. They are in a park. The children are playing with a ball. The mother is sitting on a bench and she is reading a book. The father is... he is standing near the children. I think it is summer because the sun is shining and the trees are green. The family looks happy.
