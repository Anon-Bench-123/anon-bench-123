# Speaking Sample: Presentation Transcript

**Student**: student-1, Adult learner, General English course
**Task**: Give a 2-3 minute presentation about a hobby or interest. Explain what it is, why you enjoy it, and recommend it to the audience.

---

## Transcript of Student's Presentation

OK so today I would like to talk about my hobby which is mountain hiking. I have been doing hiking since about three years and it has become very important part of my life.

First I want to explain why I started. Actually my company has a hiking club and my colleague invited me to join one day trip to Mount Takao. At the beginning I thought it will be very tiring, but when I reached the top, the view was so amazing and I felt very refreshing. After that experience I decided to continue.

The reason I enjoy hiking is several. First, it is very good for health. When you walk in nature, you can reduce your stress and also it is good exercise for the body. Second, I like the challenge. Each mountain is different and you must prepare carefully. For example, last month I climbed Mount Tanigawa which is quite dangerous mountain. The weather changed very suddenly and we had to decide quickly if we continue or go back. We decided to go back and I think it was good decision because safety is most important.

Also hiking is wonderful way to meet people. On the mountain trail you often talk with other hikers and everyone is very friendly. I have made several good friends through this hobby. Sometimes we plan trips together to different prefecture.

If you have never try hiking before, I really recommend it. You don't need expensive equipment at beginning. Just comfortable shoes and water bottle is enough. Maybe you can start from easy trail near Tokyo, like Mount Takao or Okutama area. I guarantee you will enjoy it.

Thank you for listening. Do you have any question?
