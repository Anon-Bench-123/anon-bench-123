# Speaking Assessment: Presentation on Remote Working

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Give a short presentation in class about remote working

---

## Student Work

So, remote working is becoming very popular in these days. Many companies they are allowing employees to work from home. I think this is a good thing because, first of all, you can save time from commuting. Also you don't need to spending money on transport. However, there are some disadvantages. For example, some people they feel lonely when working from home. And also it is difficult to separate between work and personal life. In my opinion, I believe remote working is the future but companies should to provide support for their employees.
