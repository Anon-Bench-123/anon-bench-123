# Speaking Sample: Talking About School Life

**Student**: student-1, age 14, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: In a speaking assessment, the student was asked to talk for about 2 minutes about their school life, including their favourite and least favourite subjects and what they want to do after school. This is a written transcript, so only grammar, vocabulary and content can be commented on, not pronunciation or fluency.

---

## Transcript of Student's Response

OK so I want to talk about my school. I study in a public school in San Jose. Every day I wake up at six and I take the bus to arrive to school at seven thirty.

My favourite subject is science because I like very much to do experiments in the laboratory. Last week we made an experiment with plants to see how they grow with different types of water. It was very interesting. My teacher is very good, she explain things in a way that we can understand easy.

The subject I like less is mathematics. I think it is very difficult, especially the algebra. I always get confuse with the formulas. My mother helps me with the homework but sometimes also she doesn't understand. I need to study more but it is hard to find the motivation.

After school I want to study in the university. I am dreaming to become a biologist because I love the nature and the animals. Costa Rica has many beautiful animals and forests and I want to help to protect them. My father says that it is a good career because the environment is very important today.

In my free time after school I usually play football with my friends or I watch videos on YouTube. Sometimes I practice my English by watching films without subtitles, but it is difficult for me to understand everything.
