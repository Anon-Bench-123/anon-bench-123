# Speaking Assessment: Peer Debate on Online Shopping vs Stores

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Peer debate exercise on the topic 'Is online shopping better than going to stores?' (arguing in favour of stores)

---

## Student Performance Transcript

I think going to stores is better than online shopping. When you go to a store, you can touch and try the things before you buy. For example, when I buy clothes, I always want to try them first because the size can be different. Also, going shopping with friends is fun and you can spend time together. Another reason is that sometimes online shopping has problems. The delivery can be late or the product is different from the picture. But I understand that online shopping is convenient for busy people who dont have time.
