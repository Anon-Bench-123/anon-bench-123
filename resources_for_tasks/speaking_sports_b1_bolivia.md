# Speaking Sample: Describing a Sports Experience

**Student**: student-1, age 14, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Your teacher asked you to record yourself talking about a sport you play or watch. This is a written transcript, so only grammar, vocabulary and content can be commented on, not pronunciation or fluency.

---

## Student's Transcript

I want to talk about football because it is my favourite sport and I play it since I was very little, maybe six years old. In my country everybody love football and it is very popular.

I play in a team with my school and we have training two times in the week, on Tuesday and Thursday. Our coach his name is Mr Romero and he is very strict but also he teach us many things. He always say that we need to be more discipline.

Last month we have an important match against another school from Cochabamba. I was very nervous because many people was watching. In the first half we were losing two to zero and everyone was very sad. But in the second half we played more better and my friend Carlos he scored one goal. Then in the last ten minutes I received the ball and I shoot very hard and... goal! We tied the match two to two.

After the match our coach said he was proud of us because we didnt give up. He said we have to keep practice more hard. I agree because I want that my team win the championship this year. Football is not only a sport for me, is like a passion. It teaches me to work with others and to not surrender when things are difficult.
