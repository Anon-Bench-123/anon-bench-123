# Speaking Sample: Transcript

**Student**: student-1, Adult learner
**Task**: Describe a memorable journey or trip you have taken. Where did you go, what did you do, and what was the best part? (2-3 minutes)

---

## Student's Transcript

OK so I want to talk about a trip I made to Lake Khuvsgul which is in the north of Mongolia. I went there with my family last summer in August. It was very beautiful place and I think it is one of the most beautiful places in my country.

We drive from Ulaanbaatar and it take us about twelve hours by car. The road was not always good and sometimes it was only a dirt road with no surface. But I did not mind because the views from the window they were amazing. We see many animals on the way, horses and yaks and also some camels.

When we arrive to the lake, we stay in a ger camp near the water. A ger is a traditional Mongolian tent and it is very comfortable inside because it have a wood stove for keep warm. The nights were very cold even in summer so the stove was necessary.

Every day we do different things. We ride horses along the lake and we also take a small boat on the water. The water is so clear that you can see the bottom even when it is very deep. I also walk in the forest near the lake and I see many interesting birds. My daughter she was very excited because she never see so many trees before because in Ulaanbaatar there is not so many.

The best part for me was to sit by the lake in the evening and watch the sunset. It was very peaceful and quiet, very different from the city. I think everyone should visit this place one time in their life.
