# Speaking Sample: Describing a Travel Experience

**Student**: student-1, age 21, University English course
**Task**: In a speaking exam, the student was asked to describe a memorable trip they have taken. They had 1 minute to prepare and then spoke for approximately 2 minutes. This is a written transcript, so only grammar, vocabulary and content can be commented on, not pronunciation or fluency.

---

## Transcript of Student's Response

I want to talk about my trip to Antalya which I did last summer with my family. We drived there from Ankara and it was taking about six hours on the road.

When we arrived the weather was very hot, like maybe forty degrees. We stayed in a hotel which was next to the beach. The hotel had a swimming pool also and a restaurant which the food was very nice.

Every morning we went to the beach and we swam in the sea. The water was so blue and clean, it was like a picture. My younger sister was afraid from the sea because she cannot swim well, so she played in the pool most of the time.

One day we visited the old city of Antalya. There was many beautiful old buildings and small shops. I buyed a leather bag for my mother as a gift. We also ate ice cream which is famous there. The shopkeeper was very friendly and he gave us extra because he said we are good customers.

The thing I most enjoyed was the boat trip. We took a boat and they showed us some caves near the coast. The guide explained about the history but I did not understand everything because my English was not so good at that time. Actually, the guide was speaking in Turkish and English both.

I think travelling is one of the best things because you can learn about different places and meet new peoples. I want to visit Antalya again in the future, maybe with my friends next time.
