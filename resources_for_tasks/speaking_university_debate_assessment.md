# Speaking Assessment: Impromptu Speech on Free University Education

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Give a 3-minute impromptu speech on the topic 'Should university education be free?'

---

## Student Performance Transcript

Well, this is a really interesting question. I think... personally I believe that university education should be free, or at least much cheaper than it is now. In many countries, students have to take out huge loans to pay for their education, and then they spend years after graduation trying to pay back this debt. This creates a lot of stress and it means that only rich families can afford to send their children to the best universities.

On the other hand, universities need funding to maintain quality. If the government pays for everything, the money has to come from somewhere, which means higher taxes. And some people argue that if students dont pay anything, they might not value their education as much.

But I still think the benefits outweigh the costs. An educated population is good for the whole economy, so in a way, free education pays for itself in the long run.
