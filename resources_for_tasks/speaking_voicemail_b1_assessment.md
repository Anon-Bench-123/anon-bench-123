# Speaking Assessment: Professional Voicemail

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Leave a voicemail for a client explaining a delivery delay (role-play exercise)

---

## Student Work

Hello, this is [Student] calling from Nordic Solutions. I am calling to inform you about a problem with your order. Unfortunately the delivery will be delay because of a problem in the warehouse. We are very sorry for this inconvenient. The new delivery date will be on next Wednesday. If you have any questions you can call me back in this number. Again I apologise for the delay and I hope this will not cause you too much problems. Thank you and have a good day.
