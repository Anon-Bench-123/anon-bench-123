# Speaking Sample: Describing a Weekend

**Student**: student-1, age 28, Adult General English course
**Task**: The teacher asked the student to talk for 1-2 minutes about what they did last weekend. This is a written transcript, so only grammar, vocabulary and content can be commented on, not pronunciation or fluency.

---

## Transcript of Student's Response

Last weekend was very nice for me. On Saturday morning I cleaned my flat because it was very dirty. After I finish cleaning, I go to the market near my house. I buyed fresh vegetables and fruits because I wanted to cook something special.

In the afternoon my friend Anna come to visit me. We cooked together a soup, it is called goulash. It is very popular in Hungary. We putted many paprika and meat inside. It was tasting very good. After lunch we watched a film on Netflix. I don't remember the name but it was about a woman who travel around the world.

On Sunday I waked up late, maybe at eleven. I eated breakfast slowly and then I readed my book. The weather was bad so I stayed at home all day. In the evening I called my mother on the phone. She told me about her garden and the flowers. I feel a little boring because I don't do anything special but it was also nice to rest.
