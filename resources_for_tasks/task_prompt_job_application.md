# Writing Task Prompt

**Level**: B1
**Task type**: Formal email

---

## Instructions

You have seen this advertisement in a local newspaper:

> **WAITERS/WAITRESSES WANTED**
>
> Busy city-centre restaurant looking for full-time and part-time waiters/waitresses.
> Experience preferred but not essential. Must be friendly, reliable, and available for evening and weekend shifts.
>
> Apply by email to: manager@restaurant.com

Write an email applying for this job. In your email you should:

- say where you saw the advertisement
- describe your relevant experience
- explain why you would be good for this job
- say when you are available to work

Write 150-200 words. Use an appropriate formal style.
