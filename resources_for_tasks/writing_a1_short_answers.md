# Writing Sample: Short Answers

**Students**: student-1, student-2, student-3 (Age 9-10, Primary English class)
**Task**: Answer each question in one or two sentences.

---

## Question 1: What is your favourite food?

**student-1:** My favourite food is chicken rice. I eat it every day with my family.

**student-2:** I like is noodles. My mother cook noodles every Saturday.

**student-3:** Favourite food mango. It is sweet I like very much.

---

## Question 2: What do you do after school?

**student-1:** After school I play football with my friends. Sometimes I do homework first.

**student-2:** I am watch TV after school. Then I play game on phone.

**student-3:** After school I go home. I play my sister.

---

## Question 3: Describe your classroom.

**student-1:** My classroom is big and has many desks. There are picture on the wall.

**student-2:** My classroom have 30 chair and 15 table. Teacher desk is in front.

**student-3:** Classroom is nice. Has window and fan but no air con.

---

## Question 4: What do you like to do for fun?

**student-1:** I like to play football and ride my bike. I play with my friends every day.

**student-2:** I like swim and play basketball. My brother play with me.

**student-3:** I like drawing. I draw cat and flower.
