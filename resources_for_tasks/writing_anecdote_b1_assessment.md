# Writing Assessment: Narrative Anecdote

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a short anecdote about an embarrassing experience, practising narrative tenses

---

## Student Work

Last week something very embarrassing happen to me. I was walking to the office when I saw my boss across the street. I waved to him but then I realized it wasnt my boss, it was a complete stranger! The man he looked at me confused and I felt so ashamed. I was trying to pretend that I wave to someone behind him but I dont think he believed me. When I told this story to my colleagues they all laughed. Now they call me the man who waves to strangers.
