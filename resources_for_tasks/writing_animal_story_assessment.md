# Writing Assessment: Animal Story

**Student**: A2 child, aged 10-12, Athens, Greece
**Task**: Creative story about animals
**Key Issues**: Past simple irregular verbs, question word order, spelling of common words

---

## Student's Story

The Adventure of Max the Dog

Once upon a time, there was a dog. His name was Max. Max lived in a small house with his family. He was very happy dog.

One day, Max waked up early. He was hungry. He go to the kitchen but his bowl was empty. Where was his food? Max thinked that maybe his owner forgot to feed him.

Max decided to go outside to find food. He opened the door with his nose and he run to the garden. In the garden, he seed a cat. "Do you seen any food?" Max asked the cat. The cat shaked her head.

Max walked to the park. He was very hungry now. Suddenly, he finded a big bone under a tree! Max was so happy. He taked the bone and he bringed it home.

When Max arrived home, his owner was there. "Max! Where you were?" she asked. "I was worry about you!" She gived Max his breakfast. Now Max had his breakfast and also he had the bone from the park!

Max was the happiest dog in the world. He eated his food and then he sleeped with his bone.

The End.
