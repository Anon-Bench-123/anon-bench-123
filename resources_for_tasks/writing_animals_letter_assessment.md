# Writing Assessment: Letter to Magazine Editor about Endangered Animals

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Read a short article about endangered animals and then write a response letter to a magazine editor expressing their opinion

---

## Student Work

Dear Editor,
I read your article about endangered animals and I think it was very interesting. I agree that we should protect animals because they are important for the environment. Many animals are disappearing because people destroy their habitats for building. I think governments should make more national parks and stop people from cutting trees. Also, schools should teach children about animals so they will care about them in the future. If we do not act now, many species will become extinct. We all have a responsibility to protect nature.
Sincerely, Student
