# Writing Assessment: Formal Apology Email for Shipping Delay

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write a formal email to a client apologising for a shipping delay

---

## Student Work

Subject: Apology for Delay in Shipment

Dear Mr. Kapoor,

I am writing to sincerely apologise for the delay in delivering your order (Ref: #4521). Due to unforeseen circumstances in our supply chain, the shipment which was originally scheduled for 5th March will now be dispatched on 12th March.

We understand that this delay may cause inconvenience to your operations and we deeply regret this situation. To compensate, we would like to offer a 10% discount on your next order.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require any further information. We value your partnership and are committed to ensuring this does not happen again.

Kind regards,
Priya Sharma
