# Writing Sample: Formal Application Letter

**Student**: student-1, age 24, Adult English class
**Task**: Write a formal letter (150-180 words) applying for a volunteer position at an international NGO. Explain why you are interested and what skills you can offer.

---

## Student's Letter

Mirpur Road 42
Dhaka, Bangladesh

15 January 2026

Human Resources Department
Global Aid International
Dhaka Office

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing for apply to the volunteer position at your organisation which was advertised in the Daily Star newspaper on 10 January.

I am very interested in this position because since I was young I have the dream to help other people. I am currently studying Development Studies at Dhaka University and I will be graduating in June. During my studies I have learned about many issues effecting communities in rural Bangladesh, including access to clean water and education for girls.

I believe I have several relevant skills. First, I am speaking both Bangla and English fluently. Second, I have experience of working with children because I teach English at a local community centre on weekends. Third, I am very good with computers and I can using Microsoft Office and basic data analysis.

I am available to start from July 2026 and I can commit for at least 6 months. I would be grateful for the opportunity to discuss my application in more detail.

I look forward to hear from you.

Yours faithfully,
student-1
