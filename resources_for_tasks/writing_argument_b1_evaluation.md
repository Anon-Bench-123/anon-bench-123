# Writing Sample: Argumentative Essay

**Student**: student-1, Age 14, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write an argumentative essay of 120-150 words on the topic: "Should students wear school uniforms?" Give your opinion and reasons.

---

## Student's Writing

I think students should wear school uniforms because it is very important for many reasons.

First, when all the students wear the same clothes, there is no problem about who has expensive clothes or not. Some students have a lot of money and they can buy very good brand clothes, but other students cannot. If everyone wear uniform, nobody feels bad about their clothes.

Second, it is more easy to get ready in the morning. You don't need to think about what to wear every day. My mother always says me that I spend too much time choosing my clothes, so I think uniform is faster.

However, some people think that uniforms are boring and students cannot show their personality. I understand this idea but I don't agree. Students can show their personality in other ways, for example with their bag or their shoes.

In conclusion, I believe that school uniforms are a good idea because they make everyone equal and save time. Schools should make all students to wear them.
