# Writing Sample: Argumentative Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 16, Upper-Secondary
**Task**: Write an essay arguing for or against the statement "Schools should ban mobile phones completely" (180-220 words)

---

## Student's Essay

Nowadays, mobile phones are everywhere, also in schools. Some people think that schools should to ban them completely, but I disagree with this opinion. In this essay I will explain my reasons.

First of all, mobile phones can be very useful for learning. For example, students can look up words in a dictionary app or search for informations on the internet during the lesson. This is much faster than to use a paper dictionary and it helps the students to be more independent in their learning.

On the other hand, I understand that some students use their phones for playing games or chatting with friends during the class. However, I think the solution is not to ban the phones but to teach the students how to use them in a responsible way. If we ban the phones, we do not teach them nothing about self-discipline.

Furthermore, mobile phones are important for the safety. Parents want to be able to contact their children in case of emergency. If the school bans the phones, parents will be worried and they will complain.

In conclusion, I believe that banning mobile phones is not the answer. Instead of this, schools should create clear rules about when and how students can use their devices in the classroom.
