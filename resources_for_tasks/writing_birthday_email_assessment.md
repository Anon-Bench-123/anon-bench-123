# Writing Assessment: Birthday Invitation Email

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Write a short email to a friend inviting them to a birthday party

---

## Student Work

Dear my friend Patrice,
I am write to you because I want invite you for my birthday party. It will be on Saturday 15 March at my house. The party begin at 7 oclock in the evening. There will be food and music and dancing. Please you come because it will be very fun. You can bring your brother also if he want.
I am waiting for your response.
Thank you,
Marie
