# Writing Assessment: Business Email to Client

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write an email to a client confirming a meeting schedule (business writing exercise)

---

## Student Work

Dear Mr. Johnson,

Thank you very much for your email. I am writing to confirm the schedule of the meeting next week.

The meeting will be held on Tuesday, March 18th, from 14:00 to 16:00 at our Tokyo office. We will discuss about the Q2 marketing strategy and the budget allocation. I have attached the agenda for your reference.

Please kindly let us know if this schedule is convenient for you. If you need to change the date or time, we can adjust according to your schedule.

Also, I would like to confirm that Mr. Tanaka and Ms. Suzuki from our team will attend the meeting. Could you inform us who will participate from your side?

I look forward to meet you next week.

Best regards,
[Student]
