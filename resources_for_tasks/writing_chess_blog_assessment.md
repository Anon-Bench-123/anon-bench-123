# Writing Assessment: Blog Post about Playing Chess

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a blog post about a hobby

---

## Student Work

Chess is my favourite hobby and I have been playing since I was 8 years old. My grandfather teached me how to play and now I am much better than him! I play chess online almost every day and I also go to a chess club every Saturday.

The thing I like most about chess is that it makes you think very carefully about every move. You need a good strategy and you must be patient. Sometimes I lose because I make a mistake in the end of the game when I am tired.

I want to enter competitions in the future and maybe become a chess master one day. My dream is to play in a tournament in another country.
