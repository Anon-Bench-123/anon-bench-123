# Writing Assessment: Advantages and Disadvantages of City Living

## Context
**Level:** Not specified
**Task:** Write about the advantages and disadvantages of living in a city

---

## Student Work

Living in a city have many advantage and disadvantage. First, in city you can find good job and earn more money. Also there are many shop, restaurant and entertainment. The hospital and school are better in city.

But city life is not always good. The air is polluted and there is many noise. Also the rent is very expensive and traffic is terrible. Some people feel lonely in big city because they dont know their neighbor.

I think city is good for young people but not for old people or families with small children.
