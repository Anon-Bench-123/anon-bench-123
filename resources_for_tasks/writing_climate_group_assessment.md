# Writing Assessment: Group Discussion Summary

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write a joint summary of a group discussion about climate change, presenting both sides and reaching a group conclusion

---

## Student Work

Climate Change Discussion Summary

In our group we discuss about the effects of climate change. Student A said that the governments should invest more in renewable energies and stop using fossil fuels. Student B agreed but added that it is also important that individuals change their behaviors, for example by recycling and using public transports. Student C argued that the economic impact of climate policies should not be ignored because many workers depend on traditional industries.

We agreed that a balanced approach is needed. The governments should to support green energy but also help workers who loose their jobs in traditional sectors. Everyone should try to reduce their carbon footprint.

The main difficulty in our discussion was to find a compromise between environmental protection and economic growth.
