# Writing Sample: Formal Complaint Letter

**Student**: student-1, adult learner, Business English course
**Task**: Write a formal letter of complaint about a faulty product you purchased online

---

## Student's Letter

Hauptstrasse 45
80331 Munich
Germany

12 January 2026

Customer Service
TechWorld Online Store
Berlin, Germany

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to you because I want to complain about a laptop computer that I bought from your website on 28 December 2025. The order number is TW-992847.

When the laptop arrived on 3 January, I was very happy to unpack it and start using it for my work. However, after only two days I noticed some serious problems. First of all, the screen has bright spots in the left corner which are very annoying and make it hard to read documents. Additionally, the battery does not hold charge for more than 2 hours, even though in the product description it says it should last 8 hours. This is a big problem because I need the laptop for business trips.

I tried to contact your customer hotline on 5 January to report these issues, but after waiting for 45 minutes nobody picked up the phone. I also send an email to your support team on the same day, but I have not received any answer yet. I find this very frustrating and it is not what I expected from a company that says it has "excellent customer service" on its website.

If I would have known that the quality is so poor, I would never have bought this product. I am requesting a full refund or a replacement with a working laptop. I believe this is fair because the product clearly does not match what was advertised.

I would be grateful if you could respond to this letter within 10 working days. If I do not hear from you, I will have no choice but to contact the consumer protection office.

I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Yours faithfully,
[student-1]
