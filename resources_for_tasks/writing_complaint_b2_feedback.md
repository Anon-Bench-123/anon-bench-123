# Writing Sample: Formal Letter of Complaint

**Student**: student-1, Adult learner
**Task**: Write a formal letter to a hotel manager complaining about problems during your recent stay

---

## Student's Letter

Dear Sir or Madame,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction about the service during my recent stay at your hotel from 15 to 18 January. I had booked a superior double room through your website and was very looking forward to a relaxing weekend, however the experience was far from my expectations.

Firstly, when I arrived to the hotel at 3 PM, I was told that my room was not ready yet and I should wait in the lobby. I was not offered any refreshment or apology during this time. After nearly one hour waiting, I was finally brought to my room, which was on the ground floor despite I had requested an upper floor for the view.

Furthermore, the room itself was not clean to an acceptable standard. There was hairs in the bathtub and the towels had an unpleasant smell. I complained about this issues at the reception and was promised that someone would come to clean, but nobody was coming until the next morning.

The breakfast was the only positive aspect, which was varied and fresh. However I was not able to enjoy the swimming pool because it was closed for maintenance during my entire stay. This information was not communicated to me before my arrival.

I believe that I am deserved a partial refund for the inconvenience I have experienced. I would also appreciate to receive a written response within 14 days explaining what steps you will take to prevent such situations in the future.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
Student-1
