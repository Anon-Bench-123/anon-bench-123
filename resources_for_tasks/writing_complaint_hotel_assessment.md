# Writing Assessment: Formal Complaint Email

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a formal complaint to a hotel about a bad experience

---

## Student Work

Dear Sir,
I writing to complain about my stay in your hotel last week. The room was very dirty and the bed was not comfortable. Also the wifi was not working properly. When I asked the reception they said they will fix it but nobody came. I waiting for two hours and nothing happened. I think you should give me discount for next time. I hope you will solve this problem.
Thank you
[Student]
