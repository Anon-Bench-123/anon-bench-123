# Writing Assessment: Job Application Cover Letter

**Student**: B1 adult, aged 20-30, Quito, Ecuador
**Task**: Cover letter for job application
**Key Issues**: Modal verbs for politeness, present perfect for experience, overly direct expressions

---

## Student's Cover Letter

Dear Hiring Manager,

I write this letter because I want to apply for the Customer Service Representative position at your company that I saw on your website.

I am 24 years old and I finished my degree in Business Administration from Central University last year. During my studies, I learn about customer service and business communication. I was always interested in working with customers and solving their problems.

I worked at a call center for two years. In this job, I answered phone calls from customers and I helped them with their questions about products. I dealt with many difficult situations and I solved customer complaints. I think I am good at this job because I am patient and I understand people's problems.

I can speak Spanish and English fluently. I also know how to use Microsoft Office and CRM software. I learned these skills in my previous job and in university.

I want this position because your company is very well-known and I think it is a good opportunity for my career. I can start working immediately if you hire me. I am available for an interview any day you want.

I expect your positive response soon.

Regards,
Andrea Morales
