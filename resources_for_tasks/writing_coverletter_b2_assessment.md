# Writing Assessment: Cover Letter for Internship

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write a cover letter for a mock job application for a summer internship at an international marketing company

---

## Student Work

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to apply for the summer internship in your company. I am a student of 16 years old and I study at Lycee Victor Hugo in Paris. I am very interested in marketing because I think it is a fascinating domain.

I have several qualities which make me a good candidate. First, I am very creative and I like to find new ideas. Second, I speak French, English and a little bit of Spanish which is useful for an international company. Third, I am responsible and I always finish my works on time.

I have experience because last summer I helped in the shop of my uncle. I learned to talk with clients and to organise the merchandise. This experience taught me the importance of communication in business.

I am available from July 1st until August 31st. I hope you will consider my application. I am looking forward to hear from you.

Sincerely,
[Student]
