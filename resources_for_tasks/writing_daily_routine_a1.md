# Writing Sample: Daily Routine

**Student**: student-1, age 12, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write 5-8 sentences about your daily routine on a school day

---

## Student's Writing

Every day I wake up at 7 oclock. I eat the breakfast with my family. My mother she make pancakes. Then I go school by bus. The bus it take 20 minutes. In school I have many lessons. My favourite lesson is the music because I play piano. After school I do my homeworks and then I play with my friend Matas. I go to bed at 9 oclock. I like my school day very much.
