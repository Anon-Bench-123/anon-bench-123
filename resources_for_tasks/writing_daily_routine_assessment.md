# Writing Assessment: Daily Routine

**Student**: A2 adult, aged 22-35, Phnom Penh, Cambodia
**Task**: Short paragraph describing daily routine
**Context**: Evening language school, common errors with present simple third-person -s and time expressions

---

## Student's Writing

My name is Sophea. I work in office in Phnom Penh. I want describe my daily routine.

Every morning, I wake up at 6 o'clock. First, I take shower and get dressed. Then I eat breakfast. Usually I eat rice soup or noodles. My husband, he wake up at 6:30. He eat breakfast with me.

At 7 o'clock, we leave home. I take bus to my office. The bus is always crowded in morning. I arrive to office in 8 o'clock. My work start at 8:30.

At work, I answer emails and talk to customers. Sometimes I have meeting with my team. At lunchtime, I eat with my colleagues. We usually go to restaurant near the office.

I finish work at 5 o'clock in afternoon. After work, I go shopping or I go straight home. When I arrive home, I help my mother cook dinner. We eat dinner at 7 o'clock in the evening together with my family.

After dinner, I watch TV or I read book. Sometimes I study English. I go to bed at 10 o'clock usually.

This is my daily routine from Monday to Friday. On weekends is different.
