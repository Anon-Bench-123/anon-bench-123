# Diagnostic Writing Samples

**Class**: New lower-secondary students, ages 12-13
**Task**: Write 5-6 sentences about your family
**Context**: First week of term diagnostic writing task, Cairo, Egypt

---

## Student 1: Ahmed

My family is big. I have three brother and two sister. My father he work in bank. My mother she is teacher. I love my family very much. We live in Cairo.
