# Writing Assessment: Paragraph on the Importance of Education

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a paragraph about the importance of education as part of a classroom writing exercise

---

## Student Work

Education is very important for everyone in the world. Without education, people cannot get good jobs and they will be poor. In my country, many children in villages do not go to school because their families need them to work. This is a big problem because these children will not have opportunities in the future. I believe that the government should build more schools in rural areas and give free books to students. Education can change someone's life and make society better for all people.
