# Writing Sample: Informal Email

**Student**: student-1, age 14, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write an email to your English-speaking friend telling them about your weekend

---

## Student's Email

Subject: My Weekend!

Hi Sarah,

How are you? I want to tell you about my weekend. It was very nice!

On Saturday I wake up late because no school. In the morning I help my mother to clean the house. After I eat lunch I go to the souk with my cousin Fatima. The souk was very beautiful and we look at many things. I buyed a new bag for school. It is blue color. Fatima she buyed shoes. We drink mint tea in a cafe and we talked about our lifes.

On Sunday I stayed at home. I watched a film in English but I didn't understood all the words. The film was about a girl who travel around the world. It was very excited! After I do my homework and then I play football with my brother in the street.

I hope you had a good weekend to. Write me soon and tell me about your weekend!

See you,
student-1
