# Writing Sample: Informal Email

**Student**: student-1, age 16, Upper-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write an email to your English-speaking pen pal (60-80 words) telling them about your weekend plans.

---

## Student's Email

Hi Tom,

How are you? I hope you are fine. I write to you because I want to tell about my plans for the weekend.

On Saturday, me and my family will going to cabin in the mountains. We have a cabin near Lillehammer. I am very exciting because we will make ski all day. My father he is very good in skiing and he will teach me some new tricks.

On Sunday we make a big dinner with grandparents. My grandmother she always cook fish and it is very delisious.

What will you do this weekend? Write me soon!

Best regards,
student-1
