# Writing Sample: Semi-formal Email

**Student**: student-1, age 15, Upper-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write an email (100-130 words) to a new exchange student who is coming to your school next month. Welcome them, tell them about the school, and suggest something to do together.

---

## Student's Writing

Subject: Welcome to our school!

Hi James,

My name is student-1 and I am student in your new class. I am very exciting that you are coming to our school in Zagreb! I want to tell you some things about our school.

Our school is not very big but it is very nice. We have about 400 students and the teachers are mostly friendly. The lessons start at 8 o'clock and finish at 2 o'clock. We have a break at 10 for eating something. The cafeteria has good food but it is always very crowd. My favourite subjects are English and biology.

I think we can go together to the center of Zagreb after school one day. There is many interesting things to see, like the main square and the old buildings. We can also try some Croatian food if you want. I recommand the burek, it is really good!

I am looking forward to meet you!

Best wishes,
student-1
