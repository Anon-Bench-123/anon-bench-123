# Writing Sample: Informal Email

**Student**: student-1, age 15, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write an email (100-120 words) to your English-speaking friend telling them about a recent school trip. Include where you went, what you did, and whether you enjoyed it.

---

## Student's Email

Hi Jake,

How are you? I want to tell you about my school trip what we had last week. We went to Brasov, it is a beautiful city in the mountains of Romania.

First we visited the old town and we see many old buildings and churches. The guide was very interesting and told us about the history. After that we go to a restaurant and we eat traditional Romanian food. I try sarmale for first time and it was delicious!

In the afternoon we walked in the nature around the city. The views was amazing. I take many photos which I want to show you.

I really enjoyed this trip because it was very funny and I learned many new things. The only problem was the bus trip which was very long, about four hours, and I get a little bit sick.

I hope you are good. Write me back soon!

Best wishes,
student-1
