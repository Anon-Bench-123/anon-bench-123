# Business Email Writing Samples - B2 Professionals (Seoul, South Korea)

## Context
**Level:** B2 | **Learners:** 3 Korean professionals | **Task:** Business email writing

---

## Email Sample 1: Request for Meeting

### Student: Kim Minjun (Sales Manager)

**Original Email:**

**Subject:** meeting

Dear Mr. Johnson,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to you because I want to discuss about our new product line. Our company has developed innovative software solutions that I believe would be very beneficial for your company's operations.

I would like to arrange a meeting with you at your convenience. Please let me know when you are available. I can come to your office any time next week.

I am looking forward to hearing from you soon.

Best regards,
Kim Minjun

## Email Sample 2: Complaint Email

### Student: Park Jisoo (Procurement Manager)

**Original Email:**

**Subject:** Problem with order

Dear Supplier,

We ordered 500 units of product XYZ on March 1st, but we still didn't receive them. This is causing big problems for us because we promised our customers to deliver by March 20th.

You told us the shipment would arrive March 15th, but today is March 22nd and nothing arrived yet. This is not acceptable. We are very disappointed with your service.

Please send the products immediately or we will cancel the order and find another supplier.

Regards,
Park Jisoo

## Email Sample 3: Thank You Email

### Student: Lee Hyejin (Marketing Coordinator)

**Original Email:**

**Subject:** Thank you

Dear Ms. Anderson,

Thank you very much for meeting me yesterday. It was very nice to meet you and learn about your company.

I am very interested in the collaboration opportunity you mentioned. I think it would be good for both our companies.

Thank you again.

Best regards,
Lee Hyejin
