# Writing Sample: For-and-Against Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 16, Upper-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a short for-and-against essay (120-150 words) on the topic: "Should plastic bags be banned in supermarkets?"

---

## Student's Writing

Nowadays, plastic bags is a big problem for the environment. Some people think that we should banned them from supermarkets, but others disagree. In this essay I will discuss both sides.

On the one hand, plastic bags are very bad for nature. They take hundreds of years to decompose and many animals like turtles and fish can die if they eat them. Also, there is too much plastic in the ocean already. If we ban plastic bags, it will help to reduce this problem.

On the other hand, plastic bags are very convenient. People use them for carry their shopping and also for put the garbage at home. If we ban them, people will need to buy other types of bags which is more expensive.

In my opinion, I think plastic bags should be banned because the environment is more important that convenience. We can use bags of cloth instead.
