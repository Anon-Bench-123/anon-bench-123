# Writing Sample: Argumentative Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 17, Upper-Secondary
**Task**: Write an essay (200-250 words) arguing for or against the statement: "Schools should teach students about climate change in every subject, not just science."

---

## Student's Essay

Climate change is the biggest problem that our world is facing today. I strongly agree that schools should teach about climate change in every subject and not only in science classes. There is several reasons for this opinion.

Firstly, climate change is affecting all areas of our lifes. In history class, students can learn about how the climate has change over centuries and what caused it. In mathematics, they can analyse the data and graphs about temperatures rising. Even in English class, students can read articles and write essays about environment issues. This shows that climate change is not only a science topic.

Secondly, when students hear about the same problem from many different angles, they understand it more deep. A student who only learns about carbon dioxide in chemistry might not understand why it matters for their daily life. But if they also discuss about it in geography and economics, they will see the full picture.

However, some people argue that teachers of other subjects they are not qualified to teach about climate change. While this concern has some validity, teachers do not need to be climate scientists. They just need to connect their subject to real-world issues.

In conclusion, I believe that teaching climate change across all subjects would help students to become more aware and responsible citizens. We cannot afford to wait, because the environment it is getting worse every year.
