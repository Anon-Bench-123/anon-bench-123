# Writing Sample: Argumentative Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 17, Upper-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write an essay (150-200 words) arguing whether developing countries should prioritise economic growth or environmental protection. Include arguments for both sides and state your own view.

---

## Student's Essay

In todays world, developing countries face a difficult challenge. Should they focus on growing their economy or protecting the environment? This essay will discuss both sides and give my perspective.

On one hand, economic growth brings many advantages. When a country develops its industry, it creates jobs and reduces poverty. People can access better healthcare and education. For example, many countries in Asia have lifted millions out of poverty through industrialisation. If developing countries do not grow economically, their people will continue to struggle.

On the other hand, environmental destruction has serious consequences. Deforestation and pollution damage the health of citizens and destroy natural habitats. Climate change, which is largely caused by industrial activities, effects everyone but especially poor communities who lack the resources to adapt.

In my opinion, these two goals are not necessarily contradictory. Developing countries can pursue what economists call "green growth" -- investing in renewable energy and sustainable agriculture rather than following the same polluting path that developed nations took. This approach allows for economic development while preserving the environment for future generations.

In conclusion, I believe that developing countries should prioritize sustainable growth that balances economic needs with environmental responsibility.
