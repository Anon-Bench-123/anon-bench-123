# Writing Sample: For-and-Against Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 20, Tertiary
**Task**: Write a short for-and-against essay on "Should university education be free?" (approximately 200 words)

---

## Student's Essay

Should University Education Be Free?

Education is one of the most important things in our society. I believe, that university education should be free for everyone because it gives people equal opportunities.

Firstly, free education means that students from poor families can also study at university. I think it's totally unfair that some students have to pay so much money just because they want to learn. Actually, in Germany, students must pay semester fees, but in other countries tuition fees are much higher. Lots of students can't afford it anyway and they have to work while studying which makes everything harder.

Furthermore, when more people have a university degree, the whole society benefits. Countries with free education systems have often a higher level of innovation and economic growth.

On the other hand, some people say that free education costs too much money for the government. This is true. Also, they argue that students who pay for their education are more motivated to finish their studies.

In conclusion, I strongly believe, that the advantages of free education are bigger than the disadvantages. Governments should invest in the future by making university education accessible to all.
