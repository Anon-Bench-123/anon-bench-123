# Writing Sample: About My Family

**Student**: student-1, age 10, Primary English class
**Task**: Write 5-6 sentences about your family. Say who is in your family, what they look like, and what they like doing.

---

## Student's Writing

My family is four people. My mother name is Jiyeon. She is tall and she have long hair. She like cooking. My father is work at office. He is very strong. I have one brother. He name is Minjun. He is small and he like play computer game. We are go to park on Sunday. I love my family very much.
