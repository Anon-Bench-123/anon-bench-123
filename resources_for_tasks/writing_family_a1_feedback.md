## Student's Writing

My Family

I have big family. My father name is Mr Park. He is work in office. My mother she is teacher. She teach in school near my house. I have one brother. His name Minjun. Minjun is 7 years. He like play games on computer. I like play with him but sometimes he is very noisy.

My grandmother live with us. She cook very delicious food every day. My favorite food she makes is rice cake. On Sunday we go to park together. Everyone is happy. I love my family very much because they is kind to me.
