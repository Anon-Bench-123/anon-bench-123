# Writing Assessment: Film Review

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write a review of a film or book they recently enjoyed

---

## Student Work

I recently watched the film "The Intouchables" and I must say it exceeded my expectations. The film tells the story of an unlikely friendship between a wealthy quadriplegic man and his caregiver from a disadvantaged background. What makes this film remarkable is the way it balances humour with deeply emotional moments without ever becoming sentimental.

The performances are outstanding, particularly Omar Sy, who brings an infectious energy to every scene. The direction is subtle yet effective, allowing the relationship between the two characters to develop naturally. One criticism I would make is that the film occasionally simplifies the complexities of disability and class, but this does not significantly diminish the overall impact.

I would highly recommend this film to anyone looking for an uplifting story that challenges social conventions.
