# Writing Assessment: Food Description

**Student**: A2 child, aged 8-10, Hanoi, Vietnam
**Task**: Short description of favorite food for class cookbook
**Key Issues**: Countable/uncountable nouns, like + verb-ing, sentence structure

---

## Student's Writing

My Favorite Food - Pho

My favorite food is pho. Pho is noodle soup from Vietnam. It is very delicious!

In pho, there are many things. There are noodle, meat, and vegetables. The noodle is white and soft. The meat can be chicken or beef. There are also onion, herbs, and bean sprout.

The soup is very hot and it has good smell. I like eat pho because it make me feel warm and happy. When I eat pho, I always put lime and chili.

My mother make pho sometimes on Sunday. She cook the soup for many hours. She put many spices. The kitchen smell wonderful when she cook pho!

I like to eat pho for breakfast. In Vietnam, many people eat pho in the morning. We go to pho restaurant near my house. The pho there is the best!

Pho is my favorite food because it taste amazing and it remember me of my family.
