# Writing Sample: Formal Letter

**Student**: student-1, Age 17, Upper-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a formal letter (150-180 words) to the director of a local museum requesting a group visit for your class. Include the purpose of the visit, preferred dates, and any special requirements.

---

## Student's Writing

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to you because I would like to organise a visit to your museum for my English class. We are a group of 25 students from the secondary school in our city and we are studying about British history this term.

We would like to come on the 15th or 22nd of March, because these are our free days from school. We would need a guided tour if it is possible, preferably in English because we want to practise our language skills. The visit should take approximately two hours.

I would also like to ask if there is a discount for student groups. Some of our students has limited budget so a reduced price would be very helpfull for us. Also, one student in our group uses a wheelchair, so we need to know if all the exhibitions are accessible.

Additionally, we are interesting in any educational materials that you could provide us before the visit, so that we can prepare in advance.

I look forward to hear from you at your earliest convenience.

Yours faithfully,
student-1
