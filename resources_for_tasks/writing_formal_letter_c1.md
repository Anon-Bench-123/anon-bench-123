# Writing Sample: Formal Letter of Complaint

**Student**: student-1, Adult learner
**Task**: Write a formal letter of complaint to an airline about a delayed flight and lost luggage (200-250 words)

---

## Student's Letter

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction regarding the terrible experience I had with your airline during my recent travel from Frankfurt to London on 8 January 2026, flight number AB4721.

First of all, the flight has been delayed for three hours without any proper explanation from the staff at the gate. We were only told that there is a "technical issue" but no further details were provided. During this waiting time, the airline has not offered any refreshments or vouchers, what I consider to be unacceptable for such a long delay.

Furthermore, upon my arrival in London, my suitcase was not on the baggage carousel. I reported this immediately to the lost luggage desk, where I was given a reference number and was told that my suitcase would be delivered within 24 hours. However, it took four days until I received my luggage, during which time I had to purchase essential items for my business meetings. I have keeped all receipts and will be expecting full reimbursement for these expenses.

I would like to point out that this is not the first time I experience such problems with your airline. As a frequent traveller who flies regularly for business purposes, I expect a higher standard of service.

I would be grateful if you could investigating this matter and provide me with a written response within 14 days. I also request a compensation according to EU Regulation 261/2004 for the flight delay.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
student-1
