# Writing Assessment: Description of Best Friend

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Write a description of your best friend

---

## Student Work

My Best Friend
My best friend name is Luca. He has 15 years old. He is tall and he has brown hairs and blue eyes. He is very funny and he make me always laugh. We go to the same school and we are in the same class. After school we like to play video games together. On weekends sometimes we go to the lake for swimming. Luca is very good in sport, especially football. I like him because he is always nice with me.
