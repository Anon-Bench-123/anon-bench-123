# Writing Sample: My Favourite Hobby

**Student**: student-1, age 13, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write about your favourite hobby (80-100 words)

---

## Student's Text

My favourite Hobby is Football. I play Football since three years and I like it very much. Every Saturday go I to training with my Team. We are training for two hours and after we drink something together.

Yesterday I have with my friends played football in the Park. It was very cold but we have fun. Last week I have scored a goal in the Match and my Team has won! My Coach says I am a good Player.

I think Football is the best Sport because it is exciting and you can play with your Friends. I want to play in a big Competition one day.
