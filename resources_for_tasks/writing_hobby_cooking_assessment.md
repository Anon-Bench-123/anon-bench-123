# Writing Assessment: Paragraph About a Hobby

## Context
**Level:** A2-B1 (estimated)
**Task:** Write a paragraph about your favourite hobby

---

## Student Work

My favourite hobby is cooking. I like to cook since I was a child because my mother she taught me. I can cook many dishes like pasta, soup and salads. Cooking make me relax and happy. When I cook I forget about my problems. I think cooking is a good hobby because you can share food with friends and family.
