# Writing Sample: Paragraph About a Holiday

**Student**: student-1, age 13, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a paragraph (60-80 words) about your last holiday or a holiday you remember well.

---

## Student's Writing

Last Tet holiday my family and I go to my grandmother house in the countryside. We travel by bus for three hour. At grandmother house we eat many delicious food like banh chung and fruit. I play with my cousin in the garden. We was very happy. My grandmother give me lucky money in red envelope. I like Tet very much because all family is together and everyone is smile. The weather was cold but we feel warm because we have each other.
