# Student Writing Sample

**Student**: Amine, age 14
**Task**: Write an email to a friend telling them about a recent holiday (120-150 words)
**Level**: B1 class

---

Subject: My holiday in Marrakech!

Hi Carlos,

How are you? I hope you are fine. I want to tell you about my holiday last week in Marrakech. It was amazing!

We went with all my family -- my parents, my sister and my two cousins. We drived there from Casablanca and it taked about 3 hours. When we arrived, the weather was very hot, more than 40 degrees!

The first thing we did was to visit the Jemaa el-Fna square. There was so much peoples there -- musicians, sellers, peoples making food. The smells was incredible. I eated tajine for dinner and it was delicious. My sister she didn't liked it because she said it was too spicy.

On the second day we went to the souks. I buyed a leather bag for my mother. The man in the shop wanted 200 dirhams but I negotiated and payed only 120. I was very proud of myself!

We also visited the Majorelle Garden which is very beautiful with the blue colour everywhere. I taked many photos.

I had a wonderful time and I want to go back soon. Have you ever been to Marrakech? You should come one day!

Write me soon,
Amine
