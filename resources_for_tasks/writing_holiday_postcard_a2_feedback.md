# Writing Sample: Holiday Postcard

**Student**: student-1, age 13, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write a postcard to your English pen pal telling them about a holiday you are on

---

## Student's Postcard

Dear Emma,

Hello! I am writting to you from Antalya. We are here since Monday and we stay in a very nice hotel near the beach. The weather is very hot and sunny every day!

Yesterday we goed to the old city. It was very beautifull and we taked many photos. My father he buyed a carpet for our house. It was very expensive but my mother said it is very nice. After we eat lunch in a restaurant. I eated a kebab and it was delishous.

Today we are at the beach. I swimmed in the sea this morning and the water was very warm. My sister she dont like swimming because she is scared of the fishes. She prefered to sit on the sand and read her book. Tomorrow we will go to a water park. I am very exciting!

I hope you are having a good summer to. I will tell you more when I come back.

Love,
student-1
