## Student Work

Dear Manager,
I am writing to complain about the service I received at your hotel last weekend. When I arrived, my room was not ready even though I had booked two months in advance. The receptionist was rude and unhelpful. She told me to wait without offering any explanation or apology.

Furthermore, when I finally got to my room, the bathroom was dirty and the air conditioning did not work. I reported this to the front desk but nobody came to fix it during my entire stay.

I expect a full refund for the two nights I stayed and a written apology from the management. If I do not receive a satisfactory response within 14 days, I will be forced to escalate this matter.

Yours sincerely,
A concerned guest
