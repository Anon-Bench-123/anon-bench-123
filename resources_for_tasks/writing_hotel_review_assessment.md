# Writing Assessment: Hotel Review

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Write a short hotel review as part of hospitality coursework

---

## Student Work

I stay at the Grand Hotel last month for two night. The hotel is very clean and the room is big. The bed was comfortable and the view from the window was beautiful. The breakfast was good, they have many food. The staff was friendly and help me with my luggage.

But the wifi was very slow and there was no swimming pool. Also the hotel is far from the city centre so I need to take taxi every day.

I think this hotel is good for families but not for young people because there is nothing to do at night. I give this hotel 3 stars.
