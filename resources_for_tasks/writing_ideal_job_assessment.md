# Writing Assessment: Description of Ideal Job

## Context
**Level:** Not specified
**Task:** Describe their ideal job (placement test writing task)

---

## Student Work

My ideal job is to be a chef in a famous restaurant. I love cooking very much since I was young. My grandmother teach me many Thai recipes and now I can cook many dishes. I want to work in a restaurant where I can create new menu and use fresh ingredients from the market. The most important thing for me is that the job must be creative and I can express my ideas through food. I also want to travel to other countries to learn about different cuisine.
