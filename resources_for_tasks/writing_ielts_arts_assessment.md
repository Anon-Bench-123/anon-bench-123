# Writing Assessment: IELTS Task 2 Essay Excerpt

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** IELTS Writing Task 2 - 'Some people think that the government should spend money on public services rather than on the arts. To what extent do you agree?'

---

## Student Work

It is often argued that government budgets should prioritize essential public services over funding for the arts. While I understand this perspective, I believe that both areas deserve adequate investment.

Public services such as healthcare and education are undeniably crucial for the wellbeing of citizens. Without proper funding, hospitals become overcrowded and schools lack the resources needed to provide quality education. Therefore, it is reasonable to argue that these services should receive priority.

However, the arts play an equally important role in society. Cultural institutions such as museums and theatres contribute to the identity of a nation and provide employment opportunities. Furthermore, the arts have been shown to improve mental health and foster creativity among young people.
