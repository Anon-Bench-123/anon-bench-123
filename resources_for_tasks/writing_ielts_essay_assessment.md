# Writing Assessment: IELTS Task 2 Essay

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a Task 2 essay on the topic: "Some people believe that children should be taught to be competitive, while others think cooperation is more important. Discuss both views and give your opinion."

---

## Student Work

In modern world, there is big debate about children education. Some people think competitive is very important for children, other people think cooperation is more better.

First, competitive can help children to success in future. When children learn to compete, they become more stronger and they can face challenges. For example, in my country many students compete for scholarship and this make them study harder.

On other hand, cooperation teach children to work together. In workplace, people need cooperation more than competitive. Also when children cooperate they learn respect each others.

In my opinion, I think both are important but cooperation is more better because in real life we need work with other people.

In conclusion, although competitive has some benefits, cooperation is more important for children future.
