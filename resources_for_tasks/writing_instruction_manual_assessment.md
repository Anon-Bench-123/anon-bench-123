# Writing Assessment: Instruction Manual

**Student**: B1 adult learner, aged 22-28, Colombo, Sri Lanka
**Task**: Instructions for using new office equipment
**Key Issues**: Imperative forms, sequencing language, clarity of instructions

---

## Student's Instructions (350 words)

HOW TO USE THE PHOTOCOPIER - MODEL XP-5000

**Before You Start**

Make sure you turn on the machine and wait until the green light is showing. The machine needs about 2 minutes for warming up. You should check that there is paper in the tray. If the paper tray is empty, you need to add paper before you start copying.

**Basic Copying**

1. You should lift the scanner lid
2. You place your document face-down on the glass
3. You need to align it with the corner marker (usually top-left)
4. Close the lid carefully so you don't move the document
5. You press the number pad for entering how many copies you want
6. After that, you should press the green START button
7. You wait for the copies to come out
8. You need to remove your original document

**Making Copies on Both Sides (Duplex Copying)**

If you want to copy on both sides of the paper, you should follow these steps:

First, you place your document like explained in Basic Copying section. Then, you need to press the MENU button. After you press MENU, you will see options on the screen. You scroll down until you find "2-sided" option. Select this by pressing it. The machine will ask if you want "1-sided to 2-sided" or "2-sided to 2-sided". You choose the one you need.

After you selected the option, press START. The machine starts copying. When it finishes the first side, maybe you need to turn over your original document if you selected "2-sided to 2-sided". The screen will tell you when to do this.

**Scanning Documents**

1. Place document on the glass like you did for copying
2. You press the SCAN button (the blue one)
3. You enter the email address where you want send the scanned file
4. You can also choosing the file format (PDF or JPEG)
5. After finishing these settings, press START
6. The machine will scans the document and sends it to your email

**Troubleshooting**

If you see error messages:
- "Paper Jam" - You should open the side panel and remove stuck paper carefully
- "Add Paper" - You need to refill the paper tray
- "Replace Toner" - You must contact the IT department

**Important Notes**

- You should never copy passports or money - this is illegal
- Always you take your original documents when you finish
- If the machine has problems, don't try fixing it yourself - call IT support

---

**Strengths**: Organized with clear sections, includes troubleshooting, covers important safety/legal notes
**Areas for Improvement**:
- Imperative forms: Should use direct imperatives for instructions, not "you should/need to" - "Lift the scanner lid" not "You should lift," "Press the button" not "You press"
- Consistency: Mixes imperative ("Close the lid"), modal constructions ("you should press"), and present simple ("you press") - should be consistent imperatives throughout
- Sequencing language: Weak transitions - "After that," "Then," "First" are basic; could use "Next," "Subsequently," "Once you have...," "Before proceeding," "After completing"
- Clarity: "you place your document like explained" (should be "as explained"), "The machine starts copying" (passive better: "The machine will start copying")
- Word choice: "for warming up" (should be "to warm up"), "you want send" (missing "to"), "you can also choosing" (should be "choose"), "will scans" (should be "will scan")
- Word order: "Always you take" (should be "Always take")
- Article usage: "contact the IT department" (correct), but "call IT support" (inconsistent - should be "the IT support team")
