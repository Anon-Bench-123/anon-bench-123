**Task**: Write a formal report (200-250 words) evaluating the effectiveness of your university's internship programme and making recommendations for improvement

## Student's Report

Report: Evaluation of the University Internship Programme

1. Introduction

This report aims to evaluate the current internship programme offered by the Faculty of Business Administration and to suggest improvements which could benefit future students. The data for this analysis was collected from a survey to which 85 students responded.

2. Key Findings

The survey revealed that, while the majority of participants considered the internship valuable for their professional development, there is significant areas of concern. Firstly, 62% of respondents reported that the matching process between students and companies was inadaquate, resulting in placements that did not align with their academic specialisation. Moreover, supervising from both university tutors and workplace mentors was described as inconsistent. Several students noted that they have received no feedback throughout their three-month placement.

3. Recommendations

Based on this findings, I would like to suggest the following improvements. The university should implement a more robust matching system that takes into account students' academic focus and career aspirations. Additionally, it is recommended that monthly check-ins between the student, workplace mentor, and university supervisor are established. This would ensure that any issues they are identified early and addressed promptly.

4. Conclusion

In despite of its shortcomings, the internship programme provides valuable professional exposure. By addresssing the structural weaknesses outlined above, the programme has the potential to become a cornerstone of the university's practical education offer.
