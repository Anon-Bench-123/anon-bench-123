# Student Writing Sample

**Student**: Valentina, age 26, Colombian Spanish L1
**Task**: Write a formal email applying for a job (see task prompt)
**Level**: B1 class

---

Subject: Application for Waitress Position

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to apply for the position of waitress at your restaurant which I saw in the newspaper last week. I am very interesting in this job because I love working with people and I have experience in this area.

I am 26 years old and I am from Colombia. Currently I am living in Bogota and I am studying English at a language school. I have been working in restaurants since 3 years. My last job was in a cafe called "El Sabor" where I worked for two years. My responsibilities included to take orders, to serve food and drinks, and to help with cleaning at the end of the day.

I believe I am a good candidate for this position because I am very responsible and hardworking. I am also very friendly and I enjoy to interact with customers. My previous boss always said that I am very good with difficult customers because I stay calm and I try to find a solution. For example, one time a customer complained that his food was cold and I apologised and brought him a new plate very quickly. He was very happy after and he left a big tip.

I can work full-time or part-time, and I am available for working evenings and weekends. I am flexible about the schedule because my English classes are only in the mornings.

I am attaching my CV for your reference. I would be very grateful if you could consider my application. I am looking forward to hear from you.

Yours faithfully,
Valentina Ramirez
