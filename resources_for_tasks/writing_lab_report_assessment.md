# Writing Assessment: Lab Report Abstract

## Context
**Level:** C1
**Task:** Write an abstract for a lab report on the effect of temperature on reaction rate

---

## Student Work

This experiment was conducted to investigate the effect of temperature on the rate of chemical reaction. We hypothesised that increasing the temperature would result in a faster reaction. The methodology involved measuring the time for the reaction to complete at five different temperatures. Our results showed that the reaction rate increased significantly when the temperature was raised. This findings are consistent with the Arrhenius equation which predicts that higher temperatures lead to more molecular collisions. We concluded that temperature has a direct relationship with reaction rate, confirming our initial hypothesis.
