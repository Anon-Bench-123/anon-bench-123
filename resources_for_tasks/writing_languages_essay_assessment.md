# Writing Assessment: For-and-Against Essay on Early Language Learning

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write a for-and-against essay about whether children should learn a foreign language from age 5

---

## Student Work

Learning a foreign language at a young age is a subject that divides opinion among educators and parents alike. There are compelling arguments on both sides of this debate.

Those in favour argue that young children's brains are particularly receptive to language acquisition. Research suggests that before the age of seven, children can absorb the sounds and patterns of a new language more naturally than older learners. Moreover, early exposure to other languages can foster cultural awareness and open-mindedness from a young age.

Conversely, opponents point out that young children may become confused when exposed to multiple languages simultaneously, particularly if they have not yet fully developed competence in their mother tongue. Additionally, the quality of language teaching for very young learners varies considerably, and poorly delivered lessons may do more harm than good.

On balance, I believe the advantages outweigh the disadvantages, provided that the instruction is age-appropriate and delivered by qualified teachers.
