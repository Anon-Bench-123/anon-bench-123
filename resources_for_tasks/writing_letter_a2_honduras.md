# Writing Sample: Informal Letter

**Student**: student-1, age 13, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a letter to a friend (60-80 words) inviting them to your birthday party.

---

## Student's Letter

Dear Maria,

How are you? I am write to you because next week is my birthday! I will have 14 years old on March 15. My mother is preparing a party at my house on Saturday March 16.

The party start at 3 oclock in the afternoon. We will have cake and my mother will make tamales. Also we play music and dance. My cousins they will come too.

Please you can come? I am very happy if you come to my party. My house is near to the school, you know where is it.

I wait for your answer!

Hugs,
student-1
