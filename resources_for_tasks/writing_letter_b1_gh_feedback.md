# Writing Sample: Letter to a Friend about a School Event

**Student**: student-1, age 16, Upper-Secondary
**Task**: Write a letter to a friend describing a school event you recently attended (120-150 words)

---

## Student's Letter

Dear Ama,

How are you? I hope you are fine. I want to tell you about the cultural day at my school. It was very exciting!

The cultural day was last Friday. The students come to school in traditional clothes and everyone is very happy. The teachers was also wearing traditional dress. First there was drumming and dancing by the senior students. The students was excited about the event. Everyone were dancing and singing.

After the dancing, we eat jollof rice and it tastes very nice. My friend she bring kenkey too and we all share it together. We danced and we sang and we ate food and it was a great day.

The headmaster give a speech about our culture and why it is important. He say that we should be proud of who we are.

I wish you was there! Write me soon and tell me about your school.

Your friend,
student-1
