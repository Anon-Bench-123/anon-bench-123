# Writing Assessment: Formal Letter to School Principal

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a letter to the school principal suggesting ways to make the school more eco-friendly

---

## Student Work

Dear Principal,
I writing to you because I think our school should be more eco-friendly. There are some things we can do.

First, we should to recycle more. In our school there is only one recycling bin but we need more bins for paper, plastic and glass. Second, we should turn off the lights when we leave the classrooms because it waste electricity. Third, I think we should plant more trees in the school yard.

If we do these things, our school will become more green and we will help the environment. I hope you consider my suggestions.

Thank you,
[Student]
