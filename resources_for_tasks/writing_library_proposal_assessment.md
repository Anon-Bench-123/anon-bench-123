# Writing Assessment: CAE Proposal for Library Improvements

## Context
**Level:** C1
**Task:** Write a proposal for improvements to a local library (CAE Writing Paper Part 2)

---

## Student Work

Introduction
This proposal outlines a series of recommendations for modernising the Biblioteca Comunale in order to attract a wider range of users and better serve the community.

Current Situation
At present, the library primarily caters to students and elderly visitors. Opening hours are limited to weekday mornings, which effectively excludes working professionals and families. The collection is predominantly print-based, with very few digital resources available.

Recommendations
Firstly, I would propose extending opening hours to include evenings and weekends. This relatively simple change would immediately increase accessibility. Secondly, the library should invest in a digital lending platform, enabling users to borrow e-books and audiobooks remotely. Finally, dedicating a section of the ground floor to a children's reading area with regular storytelling events would help attract families.
