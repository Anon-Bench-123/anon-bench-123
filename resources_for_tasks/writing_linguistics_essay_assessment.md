# Writing Assessment: Academic Essay Introduction for Linguistics Course

## Context
**Level:** C1
**Task:** Write an academic essay introduction for a linguistics course

---

## Student Work

The relationship between language and cognition has been a subject of extensive debate in the field of linguistics for several decades. Proponents of the Sapir-Whorf hypothesis argue that the structure of a language fundamentally shapes the way its speakers perceive and categorise the world, while critics contend that cognitive processes are largely universal and independent of linguistic variation. This essay will examine the evidence for and against linguistic relativity, drawing on recent empirical studies, and will argue that a weak version of the hypothesis is supported by the available data, although the extent of this influence remains contested.
