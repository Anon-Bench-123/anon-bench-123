# Writing Sample: Descriptive Paragraph

**Student**: student-1, age 13, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write a paragraph describing a place in your town or village that you like to visit (60-80 words)

---

## Student's Paragraph

I like very much to go the market in my town. Every Saturday the market is open and many peoples come to sell and buy things. There is many different food like yams and plantains and also fishes. My favourite part is the cloth sellers because the colours are very beautifull. Sometimes my mother she take me and I help her to carry the bags. The market is very noisy and busy but I enjoy it because I meet my friends there and we can talk together.
