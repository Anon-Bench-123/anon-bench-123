# Writing Sample: Narrative Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 15, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write about a memorable trip you have taken

---

## Student's Essay

My Trip to the Beach

Last year my class go to Guaruja beach for a school trip. It was the best day of my life! We take the bus early in the morning and the travel was about two hours.

When we arrived the weather is beautiful and sunny. The teacher said we can play in the sand and then we go swimming. Me and my friends was so excited! We played volleyball on the beach and after we go to the water. The waves was really big and I am a little scared at first but then I think it is fun.

For lunch we eat fish and chips at a small restaurant near the beach. The food was very delicious and not expensive. After lunch we walk on the beach and collect shells. My friend Ana find a very big shell and she give it to me as a present. I still have it in my room.

The best part of the trip was when we make a big castle of sand. Everyone in the class help and our castle was the biggest on the beach! The teacher take a photo of all of us with the castle.

We come back to school at 6 in the afternoon. I was very tired but very happy. I hope we can go again this year because it was amazing. This trip was special for me because I spend time with my friends outside the classroom and we have so much fun together.
