# Writing Sample: Narrative Paragraph

**Student**: student-1, Adult learner, General English evening class
**Task**: Write a short narrative (100-120 words) about an experience that taught you something important.

---

## Student's Writing

Last year I had a very special experience when I joined a cooking class in my city. I always think I am a terrible cook because every time I tried to make something, it was a disaster. My friend encouraged me to try this class.

In the first day, the teacher show us how to make pho. I was very nervous but I followed the instructions carefully. When I taste my pho at the end, I couldn't believe it was so delicious! The other students also said it is very good.

This experience teach me that we should not give up before we try. If my friend didn't encouraged me, I would never discovered this skill. Now I cook for my family every weekend and they always happy with my food.
