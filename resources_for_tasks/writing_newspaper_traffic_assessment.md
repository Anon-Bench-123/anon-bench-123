# Writing Assessment: Formal Letter to Newspaper about Traffic Safety

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a formal letter to a local newspaper about a community issue

---

## Student Work

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to express my concern about the traffic situation near Lycee Victor Hugo. Every morning there are many cars and buses on Rue de la Republique which makes it dangerous for students who walk to school.

Last week, a student was almost hit by a car near the school entrance because there is no pedestrian crossing. Many parents are also worried about this problem. I think the city council should install a traffic light and a zebra crossing in front of the school.

Furthermore, I would suggest creating a special zone where cars must drive slowly during school hours. This would make our journey to school much safer.

I hope you will publish this letter so that more people become aware of this issue.

Yours faithfully,
Student
