# Writing Sample: Opinion Paragraph on School Uniforms

**Student**: student-1, age 14, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write a short paragraph giving your opinion on whether school uniforms are a good idea (100-120 words)

---

## Student's Paragraph

I think uniform is good idea for schools. When students wear same clothes nobody can see who is rich and who is poor. And students look same. And nobody laughs at someone because of clothes. This is very important for children because they are very sensitive.

Also uniform help students to concentrate on learning and not think about fashion. In my school we wear uniform and I like it because is easy in morning.

But some students not like uniforms and they think uniforms are not comfortable and they want to wear own clothes. I understand this but I still think uniform is better for everybody.

In conclusion I think schools should have uniforms because they make students equal and help them focus on study.
