# Writing Sample: Opinion Paragraph

**Student**: student-1, age 14, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a paragraph (80-100 words) giving your opinion on whether school uniforms are a good idea. Give at least two reasons.

---

## Student's Writing

I think school uniforms is a bad idea. First reason, every person want to show their own style with cloths. When you must wear same cloths every day, you feel boring and you cannot express who you are. Second reason is that uniforms cost a lot of money and not all familys can buy it easy. Some students need two or three uniforms because they get dirty fast. However, some peoples think uniforms make the school looking more professional. I understand this opinion but I still thinking uniforms are not necessary. Students should have the freedom for choose what they want to wear.
