# Writing Sample: Opinion Essay

**Student**: student-1, age 14
**Task**: Write an opinion essay (150-200 words) about whether living in a city is better than living in the countryside

---

## Student's Essay

### City Life or Country Life?

Many people have different opinion about where is the best place to live. In my opinion, living in a city is much more better than living in the countryside because there are more opportunities and things to do.

Firstly, in the city you can find many shops, cinemas and restaurants. You can go out with your friends whenever you want and there is always something interesting happening. In the countryside there is nothing to do especially in the winter and young people are getting bored very quickly.

Secondly, the education in the city is better. There are more schools and you can choose the best one. Also in the city there are many courses and activities after school like learning languages or doing sports. My cousin who lives in a small village she has to travel one hour every day to go to school.

On the other hand, some people says that the countryside is more quiet and healthy. I agree that the air is more clean but I think that for young people the city is still better because we need to have access for opportunities.

To sum up, I strongly believe that city life is better for young people because of the education and the entertainment. However, I understanding that older people might prefer the countryside.
