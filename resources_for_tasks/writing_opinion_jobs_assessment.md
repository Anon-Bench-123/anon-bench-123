# Writing Assessment: Opinion Paragraph on Part-Time Jobs

## Context
**Level:** A2-B1
**Task:** Write an opinion paragraph on the topic "Should students have part-time jobs?"

---

## Student Work

I think students should not have part-time jobs because they are too young. If students work, they will not have enough time for study and for relax. Studying is more important than earning money at this age. Also, some jobs are not safe for young people. For example, working in the restaurant until late night is not good for healths. However, I think if the job is easy and not take too much time, it can be ok. Parents should decide for their children.
