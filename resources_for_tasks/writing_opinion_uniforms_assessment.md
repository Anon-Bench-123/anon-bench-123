# Writing Assessment: Opinion Essay on School Uniforms

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write an opinion essay on the topic "Should school uniforms be compulsory?"

---

## Student Work

I think school uniforms should be compulsory because there are many advantages. Firstly, uniforms make all students look equal so there is no discrimination. If some students wear expensive clothes, other students who cant afford will feel bad. Secondly, uniforms help students to concentrate on study instead of thinking about what to wear. Thirdly, uniforms represent the school and make students feel they belong somewhere. Some people say uniforms are boring and students should express themselves but I disagree because there are other ways to show your personality. In conclusion, school uniforms are important and every school should have them.
