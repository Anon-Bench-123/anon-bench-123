# Writing Assessment: Opinion Piece on Free University Education

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Timed writing assessment - write an opinion piece on whether universities should be free

---

## Student Work

In many countries around the world, the question of free universities is debated. I strongly believe that university education should be free for everyone.

The most important reason is that education is a right, not a privilege. When university costs money, only rich people can study. This creates inequality in the society. For example, in Sweden we have free university and this helps many people from different backgrounds to get higher education.

Another reason is that educated people contribute more to the economy. They pay more taxes and they start businesses. So in the long run, free education actually saves money for the government.

Some people argue that free university wastes tax money. However, I think this argument fails to see the bigger picture.
