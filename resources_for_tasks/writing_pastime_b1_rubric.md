Marking criteria for sample writing task:  pastime B1

Marking Rubric

| Criteria | A = Good | B = Pass | C = Fail |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Content | Fully addresses all parts of the task with clear, relevant ideas. | Addresses most parts of the task; ideas are mostly relevant. | Does not address the task fully; ideas are unclear or off-topic. |
| Organisation | Well-organised with clear paragraphs and logical flow. | Some organisation; paragraphs may be uneven or ideas not clearly linked. | Poor organisation; lacks paragraphing or logical structure. |
| Grammar | Good range of structures with few errors. | Limited range; some errors but meaning is clear. | Frequent errors that make understanding difficult. |
| Vocabulary | Varied and appropriate vocabulary used accurately. | Some variety; mostly appropriate vocabulary. | Very basic or repetitive vocabulary; frequent misuse. |
| Coherence & Cohesion | Ideas are clearly connected with appropriate linking words. | Some use of linking words; ideas mostly connected. | Little or no use of linking; ideas are disjointed. |
| Length | Around 150 words. | Slightly under or over the word limit. | Significantly too short or too long. |
