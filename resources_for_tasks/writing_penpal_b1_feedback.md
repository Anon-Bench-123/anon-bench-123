# Writing Sample: Informal Letter

**Student**: student-1, age 16, Upper-Secondary
**Task**: Write a letter to your pen pal describing a recent festival or celebration in your country (120-150 words)

---

## Student's Letter

Dear Sam,

How are you? I hope you are fine. I want to tell you about a festival we celebrate here in Honduras. It is call Feria Juniana and it is the most important celebration in my city San Pedro Sula. We celebrate it in June every year.

During the festival there is many activities. People dance in the streets and there is music everywhere. Also there is many delicious food like baleadas and tamales. My family and me we went to the carnival parade last week and it was very exciting. The people were wearing colourful costumes and they were dancing traditional dances.

My favourite part was the live music concerts because I love to hear music. There was a very famous singer and I was very happy because I could to see him in person. The atmosphere it was incredible.

Do you have similar festivals in your country? I would like to know about it.

Write me soon!
Student-1
