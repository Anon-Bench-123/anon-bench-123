# Writing Assessment: Descriptive Essay About an Admired Person

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Describe a person you admire and explain why

---

## Student Work

A Person I Admire

The person I admire most is my grandfather. He is a retired teacher who teached in a village school for 35 years. He always say that education is the most important thing in life.

My grandfather is very wise and patient. He never get angry even when things are difficult. When I was young he used to tell me stories about his life and I always listened carefully. He growed up in a small village without electricity and he had to walk many kilometers to reach his school.

I admire him because despite all the difficulties he never give up. He also helped many poor students in the village by giving them free tuition. Now he is old but he still reads books every day and helps children in the neighbourhood with their homework.

I want to be like him when I grow up because he is a good example of a kind and dedicated person.
