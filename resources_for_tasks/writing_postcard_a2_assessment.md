# Writing Assessment: Travel Postcard

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Write a postcard from a trip as a class exercise

---

## Student Work

Dear Anna,
Greetings from London! I am here since three days and I am having a wonderful time. Yesterday I visit the Buckingham Palace and I see the changing of the guard. It was very impressionant! Today I go to the British Museum. The weather is cold but I dont care because everything is so beautiful. English people are very polite. Tomorrow I will go to the Tower of London. I miss you and I hope to see you soon.
Love, [Student]
