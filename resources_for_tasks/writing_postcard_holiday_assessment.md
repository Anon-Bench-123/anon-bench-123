# Writing Assessment: Holiday Postcard

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Write a postcard from a holiday

---

## Student Work

Dear Sarah,
I am in Zanzibar now! The beach is so beautiful and the water is blue. Yesterday we went to the market and I buyed many souvenirs for my family. We are staying in a nice hotel near the beach. The food here is very delicious especially the seafood. Tomorrow we will go snorkeling I am very excited! I wish you were here.
Love, Grace
