# Writing Assessment: Online Product Review

**Student**: A2 teenager, aged 13-15, Thimphu, Bhutan
**Task**: Write product review for e-commerce website
**Key Issues**: Present perfect for experience, comparative forms, opinion expressions

---

## Student's Review (280 words)

**Review of Wireless Bluetooth Headphones - Model BH-200**

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4 out of 5 stars)

I buy these headphones last month and I use them every day since then. Overall, I am very happy with them!

**Sound Quality**
The sound quality is very good. The music sounds clear and the bass is strong. I like to listen rock music and electronic music, and these headphones make the music sound great. They are much good than my old headphones that I had before.

**Comfort**
The headphones are comfortable to wear. I can wear them for many hours without my ears hurting. The ear cushions are soft and nice. My friend has different headphones and his headphones are not comfortable as these ones.

**Battery Life**
The battery life is excellent! The company says the battery lasts 30 hours, and I think this is true. I charge them only one time per week even though I use them every day for 2-3 hours. This is more better than I expected.

**Design**
The design is modern and cool. I choose the black color and it looks very nice. They are also light, so they don't feel heavy on my head. However, I think the price is little expensive for students like me.

**Connection**
Connecting them to my phone is very easy. I just turn on the Bluetooth and they connect immediately. I never have problems with the connection, and the range is good - I can walk around my room while my phone is on the desk.

**Problems**
There is only one small problem. Sometimes when I fold them to put in the bag, they feel like they might break because the plastic is not very strong. I hope they will last long time.

**Conclusion**
I recommend these headphones for anyone who wants good quality wireless headphones for not too expensive price. They are perfect for students and young people.

Would I buy them again? Yes, definitely!

---

**Strengths**: Well-organized with clear sections, appropriate length and structure for online review, uses rating system
**Areas for Improvement**:
- Present perfect: "I buy these headphones last month" (should be "I bought"), "I use them every day since then" (should be "I have used/I've been using")
- Comparative forms: "much good than" (should be "much better than"), "are not comfortable as" (missing "as" - should be "not as comfortable as"), "more better" (double comparative - should be "better" or "much better")
- Opinion expressions: Basic opinion phrases ("I think," "I am very happy") - could vary with "In my opinion," "From my experience," "I would say"
- Article usage: "I like to listen rock music" (missing article - "to listen to rock music"), "for not too expensive price" (should be "for a reasonable price" or "at an affordable price")
- Minor errors: "I charge them only one time per week" (wordy - "I charge them once a week"), "little expensive" (should be "a little expensive"), "last long time" (missing article - "a long time")
