### Essay 1

Title: Should students learn life skills such as cooking and dealing with money at school?

I think students should learn life skills in the school. Cooking is important because you need eat every day. If you don’t know cook, you eat only fast food.

Also, money is very important. Many young people don’t know how use money good. They spend all and don’t save. In the school, teachers can explain how to buy things and how to save.

Some people say school is only for maths and history, but I think life skills are also important. You use them in the real life.

Students need learn how to live alone. Cooking and money are part of this. School can help with this things.

### Essay 2

Title: Is social media bad for society?

Social media is everywhere. Teenagers and adults use it every day. It helps us to connect and share.

There are many advantages. You can meet people, learn new things, and express your ideas. But there are also problems. People sometimes forget live in the real world. They spend hours in TikTok or Instagram and don’t study or do sport. Also, many people feel bad when they see perfect photos. They think their life is not enough good.

Another problem is privacy. People share too much and don’t think about the risks.

In my opinion, social media is not bad, but we need to use it with responsability. We must learn to protect our privacy and not compare with others. Social media is useful, but only if we use it well.

### Essay 3

Title: How important is it to attend after-school clubs and events?

After-school clubs and events are very important for students. They help young people to develop social skills and discover new interests.

Many students only focus in academic subjects, but clubs offer opportunities to learn things that are not in the classroom. For example, drama club can improve confidence, and sports teams teach teamwork and discipline.

Also, these activities help students to make friends and feel part of the school. This is specially important for teenagers who feel lonely or shy.

Some people think clubs are a waste of time, but I disagree. They are not only fun, but also useful for future. Universities and jobs look for people with more than just good grades.

In my opinion, attending clubs and events is a great way to grow as a person. Students should try different activities and find what they like. It’s not obligatory, but it’s very recomendable.
