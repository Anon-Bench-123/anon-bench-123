# Writing Sample: Recipe Instructions

**Student**: student-1, age 13, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a short set of instructions (80-100 words) explaining how to make your favourite food or drink.

---

## Student's Writing

How to Make Llapingachos

Llapingachos is a traditional food from Ecuador. It is very delicious and my grandmother she teach me how to make.

First, you need to boil the potatos until they are soft. Then you mash them and add cheese and onion. You mix everything together and make small balls. After that you flat the balls to make like a circle shape.

Next, you put oil in a pan and fry the llapingachos until they are gold on both sides. You need to be careful because the oil is very hot.

Finally, you serve them with salad and a fry egg on the top. My family we eat this every weekend. It is very easy to make!
