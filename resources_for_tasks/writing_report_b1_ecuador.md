# Writing Sample: Short Report

**Student**: student-1, age 22, Adult English class at university
**Task**: Write a short report (100-120 words) about the results of a class survey on favourite free-time activities.

---

## Student's Report

Report on Free-Time Activities Survey

Introduction
This report present the results of a survey about free-time activities. We asked 20 students in our class what they like to do in their free time.

Results
The most popular activity was playing sports, which 12 students choosed. The second popular activity was watching movies (8 students). Six students said they enjoy reading books and five students like to play videogames. Only three students said cooking is their favourite activity.

An interesting finding is that boys prefer sports more than girls. The girls in our class prefer watching movies and reading.

Conclusion
In conclusion, sports is the most popular free-time activity between our classmates. We recommend the university to offer more sports facilities for students.
