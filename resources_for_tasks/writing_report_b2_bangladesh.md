# Writing Sample: Semi-Formal Report

**Student**: student-1, age 26, Adult English course (ESP - Business English)
**Task**: Write a short report (150-200 words) for your manager summarising the key points discussed at a recent team meeting and recommending next steps.

---

## Student's Report

Report on Team Meeting -- 5 February 2026

Prepared by: student-1

Summary of Discussion

On 5 February, our team meeting discussed three main topics. First, we reviewed the progress of the new client project. The project is currently behind of schedule by two weeks because of delays in receiving the client's requirements. Mr Rahman suggested that we should contact the client directly to speed up this process and I am agreeing with this suggestion.

Secondly, we discussed about the budget for the next quarter. The finance department has informed us that our budget will be reducing by ten percent. This will effect our ability to hire additional staff which we had previously planned.

Third topic was the team building event planned for March. Most of the team members are preferring an outdoor activity rather than a dinner. Several peoples suggested to going to a resort near Cox's Bazar.

Recommendations

I would like to recommend the following actions. First, schedule a meeting with the client this week to clarify their requirements. Second, prepare a revised budget plan that accounts for the reduction. Third, organise a vote among team members to finalise the team building plans.

student-1
