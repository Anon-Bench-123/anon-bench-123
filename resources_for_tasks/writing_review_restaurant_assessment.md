# Writing Assessment: Restaurant Review

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a review of a restaurant for a class exercise

---

## Student Work

Last Saturday I went to a new restaurant in the city centre. The restaurant is call La Bella and it serves Italian food. When we arrived the waiter bring us the menu very quickly. I ordered a pasta with mushroom sauce and my friend she ordered a pizza. The food was really delicious and the portions were big. However the service was a little bit slow after we ordered because we waited about 40 minutes. The decoration of the restaurant was very nice with candles and flowers. I would recommend this restaurant but you should go when you have a lot of time!
