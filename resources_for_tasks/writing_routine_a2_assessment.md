# Writing Assessment: Daily Routine Paragraph

## Context
**Level:** A2
**Task:** Write a paragraph describing your daily routine using present simple

---

## Student Work

My Day
Every day I wake up at 6 oclock. I am washing my face and I eat breakfast with my family. Then I go to the school by foot. At school I am studying many subjects like maths, science and English. I am liking English very much because it is interesting. After school I am playing football with my friends. In the evening I am doing my homework and then I sleep at 9 oclock.
