# Student Writing Sample

**Student**: Kasia, age 10
**Task**: Write about your daily routine (about 80 words)
**Level**: A2 class

---

My name is Kasia. Every day I wake up at 7 o'clock. I eat breakfast with my brother. I eat bread and drink milk. Then I go to school with my friend Ola. We walk because the school is near.

At school I have many lessons. I like English and art. I don't like maths because is difficult. After school I go home and I eat lunch. My mum cook very good food. I favourite food is pierogi.

In the afternoon I do my homework and then I play with my cat. His name is Mruczek. Sometimes I watch cartoons on TV. I go to sleep at 9 o'clock.

I like my days because I am happy with my friends and family.
