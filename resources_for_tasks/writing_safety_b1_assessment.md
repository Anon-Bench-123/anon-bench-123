Safety in the Factory
Safety is very important in the factory. Every worker must to wear safety equipment like helmet, gloves and safety shoes. If someone dont wear the equipment, they can get injured. Last month one worker he fall down because the floor was wet and he break his arm. After this accident the manager decide to put more warning signs. I think safety trainings should be more often because many workers they dont take it serious.
