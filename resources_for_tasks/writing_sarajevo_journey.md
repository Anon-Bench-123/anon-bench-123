Last year I travel to Istanbul with my friend. It was my first time to fly on airplane and I was very nervous. When we arrived to Istanbul the city was so big and crowded. We take a taxi to our hotel which was in the old part of the city near the Bosphorus.

The first day we visit the Blue Mosque and the Grand Bazaar. The bazaar was amazing because there were so many shops and colours. I bought some Turkish tea and spices for my mother. The sellers were very friendly and some of them speaked Bosnian which surprised us.

The food was the best part of the journey. We tried kebabs, baklava and many other dishes. Everything was delicious and not very expensive. One evening we took a boat trip on the Bosphorus and we could see the city from the water. It was really beautiful especially when the sun going down.

The only problem was that we sometimes had difficulty to communicate because we don't speak Turkish. But most people understand some English so it was okay. I definitely want to return to Istanbul because there was still many places we didn't have time to see.
