## Student's Blog Post

My School Life

Hi everyone! Today I want to tell you about my life at school.

I go to simon bolivar secondary school in caracas. My school is big and has many student. There are about 800 students in total.

Every day, I wake up early because school starts at 7:30. Usually I am tired in the morning! I take the bus to school with my friend maria. The bus ride takes 30 minutes approximately.

At school, we study many subject. My favorite subject is mathematics because I am good at solving problem. I also like art class. In art class, we draw and paint. Our teacher, miss rodriguez, is very kind. She always help us when we don't understand something.

I don't like history because it is difficult to remember all the date and name. Also, the textbook is very boring. Sometimes I fall asleep in history class!

For lunch, I eat in the school cafeteria. Usually the food is okay, but sometimes is not very good. My friend and I often bring sandwich from home.

After school, I have activities. On monday and wednesday, I have football practice. On thursday, I go to english class. I never have free time during the week!

At home, I always do my homework before dinner. My parent say that homework is important. I often spend two hours on homework every night.

On weekends, I usually meet my friend at the shopping mall. We sometimes go to the cinema or we just walk around. On sunday, I always help my mother clean the house.

That's my school life! What about yours? Write in the comments!
