# Writing Sample: Report on a School Event

**Student**: student-1, age 14, Lower-Secondary
**Task**: Write a report about a school event you helped organise

---

## Student's Report

Report: Our School Culture Day

Last month our school has a Culture Day. Every class must prepare something about a different country. My class choose Japan. We are very exciting because we like Japanese culture.

First, we need to prepare the classroom. We put posters on the wall about Japan and we drawed the Japanese flag. Some students bring food from home. Minh's mother she cooked sushi for us but actually it was not real sushi because she use normal rice. It was still delicious! We also make origami cranes and put them on the tables.

On the day, many students and teachers come to our room. We explain them about Japanese culture. I was responsible for the presentation about Japanese festivals. I telled everyone about Hanami, that is when people go to look cherry blossoms. My friend Linh she talked about Japanese schools and she said that students in Japan they must clean the classroom by themselves. Everyone was very surprised about this.

The most popular thing was the food. Everyone wanted to try the sushi. Some students said they don't like it because it has seaweed but most people enjoy it. We also had green tea and everyone said it taste very different from Vietnamese tea.

I think our Culture Day was very successful. We learned many things about Japan and also we practiced our English. The teacher said our class did the best presentation. I am very proud of our class. I hope next year we can do it again and maybe we will choose Korea because I like K-pop very much!
