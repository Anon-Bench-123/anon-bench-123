# Writing Assessment: For-and-Against Essay on Social Media

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a for-and-against essay about social media

---

## Student Work

Social Media: Good or Bad?

Nowadays many people use social media every day. There are advantages and disadvantages of social media.

On one hand, social media help people to communicate with friends and family who live far away. It is also good for finding informations about news and events. Many young people use social media for their studies also.

On the other hand, social media can be dangerous. Some people spend too much time on internet and they become addicted. Also there are problems with privacy because companies collect personal datas. Another problem is cyberbullying which affect many teenagers.

In conclusion, I think social media has both good and bad sides. People should use it carefully and not spend too much time on it.
