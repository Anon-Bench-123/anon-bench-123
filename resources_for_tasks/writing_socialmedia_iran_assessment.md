# Writing Assessment: Social Media Advantages and Disadvantages

## Context
**Level:** B2
**Task:** Write about the advantages and disadvantages of social media (20 minutes, timed)

---

## Student Work

Social media has become an essential part of our daily lives. On one hand, it provides a platform for people to connect with others across the world and share their ideas. For instance, many students use social media to collaborate on academic projects and exchange knowledge. Furthermore, businesses can reach a wider audience through targeted advertising.

On the other hand, there are significant drawbacks. Excessive use of social media can lead to addiction and mental health issues, particularly among young people. Moreover, the spread of misinformation has become a serious concern, as it can influence public opinion and even affect political outcomes.

In conclusion, while social media offers numerous benefits, users must exercise caution and critical thinking to avoid its negative effects.
