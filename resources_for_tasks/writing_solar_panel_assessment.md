# Writing Assessment: Technical Description of How a Solar Panel Works

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a short technical description of a process (how a solar panel works) for an ESP engineering course

---

## Student Work

Solar panels work by converting sunlight into electricity. The panels are made of silicon cells which absorb the photons from sunlight. When the photons hit the cells, they knock the electrons free from the atoms. This creates an electrical current. The current is then sent to an inverter which converts it from DC to AC power. This AC power can be used in homes and buildings. Solar panels are a clean source of energy because they do not produce any pollution.
