## Student Work

The Magic Cat
One day a girl find a cat in the garden. The cat was very beautiful with big green eyes. The girl she pick up the cat and take it home. Her mother say no you cant have a cat but the girl she cry and cry. Then the cat it start to talk! It say I am a magic cat and I can give you three wishes. The girl was very surprise and she wish for a big house, a lot of money and for her mother to be happy. And they all live happy forever.
