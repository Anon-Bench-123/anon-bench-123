# Writing Assessment: Short Story - Surprise Birthday Party

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a short story beginning with the sentence 'When I opened the door, I couldn't believe my eyes.'

---

## Student Work

When I opened the door, I couldn't believe my eyes. The room was full of balloons and my friends were there. They shouted "Surprise!" and I was so happy that I almost cried. My best friend Anna organized everything. There was a big cake with candles and presents on the table. We danced and played games all evening. It was the best birthday I ever had. I will never forget this day because it showed me that my friends really care about me.
