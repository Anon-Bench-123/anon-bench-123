## Student Work

Technology change our life very much. Before people dont have phones but now everyone have smartphone. I think technology is good because we can communicate with people far away. But also technology make problems. Many children spend too much time with games and they dont study. Also some people lose their job because machines do the work. In my country many young people use internet every day for watching videos and chatting. I believe technology is helpful but we must be careful how we use it.
