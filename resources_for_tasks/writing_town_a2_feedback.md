# Writing Sample: Descriptive Paragraph

**Student**: student-1
**Task**: Write a short paragraph (60-80 words) describing the town or city where you live

---

## Student's Paragraph

My town is very beautiful town. It is in the east of my country. There are many peoples who live here. The weather is good, not too hot and not too cold. In my town there is a big market where you can buy many things like fruits and clothes and also there is a old church that is very famous. I am liking my town very much because the people is very friendly and it is a quiet place for live. On the Saturdays many people come to the market and it become very busy. I want that my town will have more parks because I like to play in the park.
