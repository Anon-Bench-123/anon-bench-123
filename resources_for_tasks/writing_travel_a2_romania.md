# Writing Sample: Holiday Postcard

**Student**: student-1, age 12, Lower-Secondary English class
**Task**: Write a short postcard (60-80 words) to your friend about a holiday you are on. Say where you are, what you have done, and if you like it.

---

## Student's Writing

Dear Maria,

I am in Mamaia with my family. We arrive yesterday and the hotel is very nice. We go to the beach every day and I swim in the sea. The water is very warm! Yesterday we eated fish in a restaurant near the beach. It was very delicous. My brother he buyed a hat because the sun is very hot. Tomorrow we will to visit Constanta city. I hope the wether is good. I am very happy here and I dont want to go home!

See you soon,
student-1
