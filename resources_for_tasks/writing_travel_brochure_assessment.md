# Writing Assessment: Travel Brochure Text

**Student**: B1 adult learner, aged 25-32, Dushanbe, Tajikistan
**Task**: Writing promotional text for tourism brochure
**Key Issues**: Persuasive language, adjective order, descriptive vocabulary range

---

## Student's Brochure Text (400 words)

DISCOVER THE BEAUTY OF TAJIKISTAN

Welcome to Tajikistan - The Roof of the World!

Tajikistan is a beautiful country in Central Asia with amazing mountains, beautiful lakes, and friendly people. If you love nature and adventure, Tajikistan is the perfect destination for you!

**The Pamir Mountains**

The Pamir Mountains are the main attraction of Tajikistan. These ancient impressive mountains offer good views and excellent opportunities for trekking and mountaineering. The Pamir Highway, one of the highest roads in the world, takes you through beautiful landscapes that will take your breath away. You can see small traditional villages where local people still live in the traditional way.

**Iskanderkul Lake**

Iskanderkul is a beautiful mountain lake located in the Fann Mountains. The legend says that Alexander the Great visited this place. The lake has blue clear water surrounded by high dramatic peaks. It's a good place for camping, hiking, and swimming in the summer months. Many tourists say it's the most beautiful place they have ever seen.

**Experience Our Culture**

Tajik culture is very rich and interesting. In our country, hospitality is very important. When you visit local families, they will welcome you with traditional food and warm tea. You can try our national dish, plov, which is made with rice delicious, meat, and vegetables. Don't miss the opportunity to visit local markets where you can buy traditional handmade carpets, ceramics, and souvenirs beautiful.

**Adventure Activities**

For adventure lovers, Tajikistan offers many exciting activities:
- Trekking in the beautiful mountains green
- Rafting in fast mountain rivers
- Rock climbing on challenging routes
- Visiting ancient historical sites old

**Practical Information**

The best time to visit Tajikistan is from May to September when the weather is nice. You will need a visa to enter the country, which you can obtain online or at the embassy. English is not widely spoken, so it's useful to learn some Russian or Tajik phrases basic.

**Why Choose Tajikistan?**

- Untouched natural beauty
- Good prices affordable for accommodation and food
- Friendly and welcoming local people
- Rich cultural heritage ancient
- Safe destination for travelers

Come and discover the hidden jewel of Central Asia! Tajikistan is waiting to show you its wonders. Book your trip today and create memories that will last forever!

For more information, visit our website or contact our tourism office.

---

**Strengths**: Enthusiastic tone appropriate for tourism, good structural organization with clear sections, includes practical information
**Areas for Improvement**:
- Adjective order: "rice delicious" (should be "delicious rice"), "souvenirs beautiful" (should be "beautiful souvenirs"), "mountains green" (should be "green mountains"), "phrases basic" (should be "basic phrases"), "prices affordable" (should be "affordable prices"), "heritage ancient" (should be "ancient heritage")
- Pattern: "blue clear water" (should be "clear blue water" - opinion before color)
- Repetitive adjectives: "beautiful" used 7 times - needs more varied descriptive vocabulary (stunning, spectacular, picturesque, breathtaking, magnificent)
- Persuasive language: Could be stronger - "good views" is weak (use "spectacular panoramas"), "nice weather" is vague (use "warm, sunny weather with clear skies")
- Redundancy: "ancient historical sites old" (ancient and old mean the same), "traditional way" after "traditional villages" (repetitive)
- Clichés: "hidden jewel," "take your breath away," "memories that will last forever" - overused tourism phrases
