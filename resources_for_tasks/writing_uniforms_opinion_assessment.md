# Writing Assessment: Opinion Paragraph on School Uniforms

## Context
**Level:** B1
**Task:** Write a short opinion paragraph (80-100 words) about whether school uniforms should be compulsory

---

## Student Work

I think school uniforms should be compulsory because they help students to focus on studying instead of thinking about clothes. Also, uniforms make everyone look equal so there is no bullying about fashion. However, some students say that uniforms are boring and they cannot express themselves. In my opinion, the advantages are more than the disadvantages. Schools should have uniforms but maybe students can choose the colour.
