# Writing Assessment: Weekend Narrative

## Context
**Level:** A1-A2
**Task:** Write about your weekend

---

## Student Work

My Weekend
On Saturday I go to the park with my dog. My dog name is Rex. He is very big and he like to run. We played in the park and I am very happy. Then we go home and I eat pizza for dinner. On Sunday I watch TV and I played with my toys. It was a nice weekend.
