# Writing Sample: Informal Email About a Weekend

**Student**: student-1, age 15, Upper-Secondary
**Task**: Write an informal email to your English-speaking friend telling them about what you did last weekend (120-150 words)

---

## Student's Email

Hi Alex,

How are you? I want to tell you about my weekend. On Saturday morning I waked up very late because the night before I was watching a film until 2 am. My mother was angry to me because I promised to help her clean the house.

After lunch I went to the city center with my friend Erik. We were looking for a birthday present for our classmate Sofia. We buyed a book because Sofia she likes reading very much. Then we went to a cafe and I drinked a hot chocolate. It was very cold outside, maybe minus ten degrees!

On Sunday I stayed home and I was studying for the English test. I was very nervious because I have not study enough. I hope I will pass it.

What about you? Did you do something fun?

Write me soon!
Anna
