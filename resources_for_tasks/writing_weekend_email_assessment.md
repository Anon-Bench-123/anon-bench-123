# Writing Assessment: Weekend Email

**Student**: A2 teenager, aged 13-15, Lima, Peru
**Task**: Short email to a friend about last weekend
**Rubric**: Task completion, grammar accuracy, vocabulary appropriateness, organization (each 5 points, total 20)

---

## Student's Email

Hi Tom,

How are you? I want tell you about my weekend.

On Saturday morning, I waked up late. Then I eat breakfast with my family. We had bread, eggs and orange juice. After breakfast, I go to the park with my friends. We play football for two hours. It was very fun!

In the afternoon, my mother and I go shopping. We bought new shoes for me because my old shoes was very small. I like my new shoes. They are blue and white.

On Sunday, I stay at home. I do my homework for school. I study math and english. Math is difficult for me. In the evening, I watch a movie with my family. The movie was about superheroes. It was exciting!

That was my weekend. What did you do?

Write to me soon.

Your friend,
Carlos

---

**Word Count**: Approximately 145 words
**Format**: Informal email to friend
