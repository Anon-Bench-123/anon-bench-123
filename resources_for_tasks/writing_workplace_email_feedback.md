# Writing Sample: Workplace Email

**Student**: student-1, Adult learner
**Task**: Write an email to your manager requesting time off for a family event

---

## Student's Email

Subject: Request for leave

Dear Mr Thompson,

I am writing for you to ask about take some days off from work. My sister is have her wedding on 15 March and I need to be travel to my home country for the celebration. The wedding will be in my hometown and it is taking about 12 hours to travel there.

I would like to request leave from Monday 13 March until Friday 17 March. I have already finish all my urgent tasks for this week and I will make sure to complete the monthly report before I go. My colleague Rania she can cover my responsibilities while I am away. I have already talked to her and she is agree.

I know this is a busy time for the department and I am sorry for any inconvenient this may cause. If there is anything you would like me to preparing before I leave, please let me to know.

I have still 8 days of annual leave remaining. Please could you to confirm if this request is approved?

Thank you very much for your understanding.

Best regards,
student-1
